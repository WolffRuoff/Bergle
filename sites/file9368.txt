							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Ensemble music
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-25503142" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-58768418" class="  clearfix">
				<p>Ensemble music includes two distinct types; music for ensembles where there is more than one instrument per part, and music for ensembles with only one instrument per part.&nbsp; An example of the former is a Symphony orchestra, where there is a violin &#39;section&#39; with many violins playing the same part.&nbsp; An example of an ensemble of only 1 instrument per part is a String quartet, where each instrument (in this case 2 violins, viola, and cello) are playing a different part.</p>

<p>Sometimes the terminology can be confusing, because we often speak of a string quartet being &quot;chamber&quot; music, but there is also such a thing as a &quot;chamber orchestra&quot; (which is smaller than a symphony, but will still have more than one instrument per part!)</p>
