					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>Early birds get the early bird specialâ€”this was true on the morning of August 24. Orientation advisors, like myself, members of Greek life, and other volunteers rose early on this Friday to help move in the Muhlenberg Collegeâ€™s class of 2016; it was certainly a special treat.</p>
<p>As each car rolled up to the dorms, we greeted them with cheers and excitement. Just as she started to wrap things upâ€¦we, the student orientation advisors, broke out in a FLASH MOB! Sounds cool right? <p>Then, I finally got to meet my orientation group! If my orientation group is a reflection of the rest of the class of 2016, we have one awesome freshman class at Muhlenberg! This marked the end of orientation. However, I cannot wait to keep in touch with the members of my orientation group and see how their Muhlenberg College experience evolves.</p>
