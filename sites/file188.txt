				<div style="color: rgb(34, 34, 34); font-family: arial, sans-serif; font-size: 12.8px;">While we are able to offer individual account access to the New York Times, we are often asked about other kinds of access to news sources. For example, we are also often asked about the Wall Street Journal.&nbsp;<span style="font-size: 12.8px;">Wall Street Journal can be accessed online (text only) via:&nbsp;</span><a data-saferedirecturl="https://www.google.com/url?hl=en&amp;q=https://search-proquest-com.muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/publication/10482&amp;source=gmail&amp;ust=1517508751750000&amp;usg=AFQjCNFKff-gdrRYc4Kg3GjuAR1ZpiiRiQ" href="https://search-proquest-com.muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/publication/10482" style="color: rgb(17, 85, 204); font-size: 12.8px;" target="_blank">https://search-proquest-c<wbr />om.muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/pub<wbr />lication/10482</a></div>

You can set up the account while on-campus or off-campus.</span></div>
