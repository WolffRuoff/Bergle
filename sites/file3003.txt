		<p><strong>HAMILTON DISTRICT MAIN STREET PROGRAM</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Organization website:</strong> <a href="http://www.downtownallentown.com">www.downtownallentown.com</a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Type of organization:</strong> Main Street Program, Allentown Chamber of Commerce</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Organizational mission:</strong> We are committed to celebrating and amplifying the transformation of Allentown into the vibrant heart of the Lehigh Valley through initiatives that advance the Hamilton District Vision. Our endeavors will engage the community in dynamic efforts to address the social, physical, economic and political realities that are critical to the Hamilton District’s growth. We will incorporate the richness of Allentown’s diversity with the Lehigh Valley’s prosperity to transform our community.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Contact person:</strong> Elizabeth Regan                <strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:elizabethr@lehighvalleychamber.org">elizabethr@lehighvalleychamber.org</a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Phone #</strong> 484-542-7827   <strong>Fax #</strong> 610-437-4907</p>
<p>1) <strong>Name and position of supervisor</strong>: Elizabeth Regan, Marketing Coordinator, Allentown Initiatives, Greater Lehigh Valley Chamber of Commerce</p>
<p><em>   Staff member who will supervise intern</em></p>
<p><em> </em></p>
<p>2) <strong>Internship location</strong>: 840 Hamilton Street, Suite 205, Allentown, PA 18101</p>
<p><em> </em></p>
<p>3) <strong>Position description:</strong> <em>Please provide a <strong>detailed</strong> description of the intern’s responsibilities. </em></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The intern will focus on a variety of tasks that promote downtown Allentown as an exciting place to live, work and play. We market downtown through social media by scheduling posts about downtown happenings on Facebook, Twitter, and our new website – www.downtownAllentown.com. Other responsibilities include, promoting and participating in the planning of our signature events, such as Blues, Brews and Barbecue; Summer in the City; First Monday, to name a few.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The intern is expected to learn about and understand the Main Street Four-Point Approach and will also be expected to attend committee meetings, special events, and the Board of Directors meetings on an as needed basis. The intern may be involved in other programs and activities pertaining to the Allentown Chamber of Commerce and the Hamilton District Main Street Program, including business seminars and events.</p>
<ol>
<li><strong> 3-4 desired organizational outcomes for this position (please use measurable outcomes when possible)</strong></li>
</ol>
<ul>
<li>An up-to-date and constantly updated website</li>
<li>Increased Allentown Merchant Program (AMP) gift card sales through marketing and other efforts</li>
<li>Consistent social media presence</li>
<li>Content for monthly newsletter, articles, photos</li>
<li>Increased organization/promotion of events-assistance in creating budgets, binders and work plans.</li>
</ul>
<ol>
<li><strong>General daily/weekly tasks</strong></li>
</ol>
<ul>
<li>Aid in planning executing special events and community projects to further the growth and development of Downtown Allentown.</li>
<li>Assist in the management of social media and website content on an ongoing basis</li>
<li>Attend meetings for HDMSP Board and Committees.</li>
<li>Provide assistance to Allentown Chamber staff.</li>
<li>Basic office duties, including photocopying, taking meeting minutes, processing AMP (Allentown Merchant Program) gift cards, etc.</li>
<li>Other duties as assigned.</li>
</ul>
<p>YOU WILL RECEIVE A STIPEND IF YOU ARE HIRED FOR THIS INTERNSHIP</p>
<p>TO APPLY: Applications are due OCTOBER 15.  Apply through the Berg Career Vault https://muhlenberg-csm.symplicity.com/</p>

		<p>Name of organization (501c3): Habitat for Humanity of the Lehigh Valley</p>
<p>Organization website: habitatlv.org</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Type of organization: Non-profit, affordable housing and community development</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Organizational mission: Habitat for Humanity of the Lehigh Valley seeks to put God’s love into action by bringing people together to build homes, communities and hope. Our vision is for a world where everyone has a decent place to live.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Contact person: Melissa Siegfried            Email: Melissa@habitatlv.org</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Phone #: 610-776-7737                  Fax #:610-776-7569</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Name and position of supervisor: Jehan Moustafa, Marketing and PR Manager </strong></li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>   Staff member who will supervise intern: Jehan Moustafa </em></p>
<p><em> </em></p>
<p>2) <strong>Internship location: 1053 Grape St. Whitehall PA 18052</strong></p>
<p><em> </em></p>
<p>3) <strong>Position description:</strong> <em>Please provide a <strong>detailed</strong> description of the intern’s responsibilities.</em></p>
<p><em> </em></p>
<p><em>Please describe:</em></p>
<ol>
<li>The project and/or programs on which the intern will focus his/her time/energy.</li>
</ol>
<p>As part of the ReStore team the Marketing Specialist will be responsible for the execution of marketing and PR strategies and initiatives to drive profitable business results at the ReStore. Working with the Marketing and PR Manager, the Marketing Specialist will develop and implement ideas and feedback in order to grow the business through the Marketing strategy and also work on integrating Habitat’s mission into the ReStore’s business model. The ReStore serves the community by collecting new or gently used building supply materials, furniture and house wares. These items are sold to the public at a discount with the proceeds supporting Habitat’s home building program in the Lehigh Valley. Every dollar sold at the ReStore saves ten pounds of trash from going to a landfill. Additionally, The ReStore recently partnered with Northampton County to initiate a latex paint recycle program, reducing the amount of paint in landfills and wastewater treatment facilities, lakes, rivers, and streams. The program encourages a responsible disposal of potentially hazardous material from entering the environment, protecting the local ecology. While the paint recycling program offers an opportunity to donate latex paint, it provides consumers with an array of colors for home improvement projects, again sold at a fraction of retail prices.</p>
<ol>
<li>3-4 desired organizational outcomes for this position (please use measurable outcomes when possible)</li>
</ol>
<p>·       Create Social media strategy and voice ·       Research and create Search Engine Optimization strategy ·       Design marketing message that incorporates mission and focus on Recycle, Reuse, Repurpose·       Develop and execute a monthly marketing schedule</p>
<ol>
<li>General daily/weekly tasks</li>
</ol>
<p>·       Manage social media accounts (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, DiggersList)·       Ensure website reflects marketing strategy and voice ·       Design marketing materials, including flyers, packaging, and store signs·       Maintain and update bulletins and advertising around the store·       Collect content for weekly ReStore E-Newsletter ·       Identify new marketing opportunities·       Communicate marketing strategy to staff and volunteers ·       Collaborate with Marketing and PR manager</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>YOU WILL RECEIVE A STIPEND IF YOU ARE HIRED FOR THIS INTERNSHIP</p>
<p>TO APPLY: Applications are due OCTOBER 15.  Apply through the Berg Career Vault https://muhlenberg-csm.symplicity.com/</p>

		<p><strong>Lehigh Mining &amp; Navigation Public Relations Intern</strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Lehigh Mining &amp; Navigation (LehighMiningAndNavigation.com), Lehigh Valley’s award-winning advertising and public relations agency, is looking for a public relations intern for the 2015 Fall Semester.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Internship job tasks include: identifying story ideas that support client objectives, writing and distributing media pitches and press releases, constructing media contact lists, following up with targeted media for prospective stories, setting up interviews, event support, working with professional public relations online software and updating client status reports.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Requirements include:</p>
<p>*14-16 hours/week availability over 2-3 days/week</p>
<p>*September 8 start date</p>
<p>*Journalism/PR experience and/or exceptional ability to organize information and write effectively</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Contact:</p>
<p>David Saba, Public Relations Director, <u>saba@LehighMiningAndNavigation.com.</u> Please include your resume and writing/press release samples when applying for the position. This is a paid internship ($8/hour).</p>
