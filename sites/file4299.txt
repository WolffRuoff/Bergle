					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>Well, I met a filmmaker today. Like a real one. Her name is Patti Bruck, and she visited Dr. David Tafler&#8217;s Exploratory Cinema class this afternoon as a good friend of the Muhlenberg professor.</p>
<p>I was able to sit in on the class, which involved a screening of her critically-acclaimed documentary &#8216;Slippage&#8217; and a talk-back from Mrs. Bruck. Just like that, the dream seemed a little more tangible. She was a human being! Just like us! She talked about her work and inquired about ours. She talked about the financial and creative processes of her career, which was entirely independent in production and creativity.</p>
<p>Needless to say, I am inspired and at the perfect time, as we have our first shoot this weekend for our short film due at the end of the semester! I will be sure to post updates!</p>
<p>Oh, and also, I had that latte around 4 o&#8217;clock and I&#8217;m still buzzing! So much energy &#8212; it had me dunking the basketball today! I might need to look into this whole coffee thing, but not really because I&#8217;m trying to stay sane!</p>
<p>Happy birthday!</p>
<p>Drew</p>
