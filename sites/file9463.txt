Advocacy-focused groups address many social justice issues&mdash;for example, global integrity, rights of indigenous peoples, and gender rights. The more operationally focused groups work on a vast array of activities, ranging from HIV/AIDS programs to education, global health, and protecting the environment. &quot;</p>
