<p><em>Infomaniac is a monthly column featuring new tools or tips related to using and finding information </em></p>



<p>It’s always a good time to check what library items are currently checked out to you. &nbsp;Simply go to the Trexler Library home page (<a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" aria-label="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/ (opens in a new tab)">http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/</a>), choose the <em>ACCOUNTS</em> dropdown on the top right and select <em>My Library Account</em>. &nbsp;Sign in with your OneLogin user name and password. A list of all items currently checked out to you and their due date will be displayed. You also can renew your Trexler Library items that are coming due by selecting the <strong>Renew </strong>button<em>.</em> There are two exceptions for renewals; from here you cannot renew past due items or interlibrary loan items. <em>To renew past due items and interlibrary loan items you will need to contact:</em><br><br>     Past Due and Billed Items &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 484-664-3500 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; circulationdesk@muhlenberg.edu<br>     InterLibrary Loan Items &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; 484-664-3510 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; illd@muhlenberg.edu </p>
