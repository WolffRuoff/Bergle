			<p><span class="photo_usage-attribution is-block" data-v-e1c1f65a="" id="attribution"><a data-v-e1c1f65a="" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/115089924@N02/45988388711" rel="noopener" target="_blank">&quot;Female sound engineer composing music&quot;</a> <span data-v-e1c1f65a=""> by <a data-v-e1c1f65a="" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/115089924@N02" rel="noopener" target="_blank">www.ilmicrofono.it</a></span> is licensed under <a class="photo_license" data-v-e1c1f65a="" href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.0/?ref=ccsearch&amp;atype=rich" rel="noopener" target="_blank"> CC BY 2.0 </a></span></p>
			</td>
			<td class="ck_border" style="border: 1px solid rgb(221, 221, 221);">
			<p>The copyright holder (usually the author) possesses these exclusive rights over her work:</p>

			<ul style="border-radius: 0px !important;">
				<li style="border-radius: 0px !important;"><strong>Reproduce </strong>(make copies of ) the work; and</li>
				<li style="border-radius: 0px !important;"><strong>Modify</strong> or prepare derivative works based on the work. (Examples of derivative works include translations, transforming printed works into musicals or films, rearrangements of scores, and any other recast, transformation or adaptation of a work); and</li>
				<li style="border-radius: 0px !important;"><strong>Distribute</strong> the work in any format by sale, publication, license, rental, or for free; and</li>
				<li style="border-radius: 0px !important;"><strong>Publicly perform </strong>or <strong>display </strong>the work; and</li>
				<li style="border-radius: 0px !important;"><strong>Authorize others</strong> to exercise some or all of those rights</li>
			</ul>
			</td>
		</tr>
	</tbody>
</table>

<p style="border-radius: 0px !important;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="border-radius: 0px !important;">&nbsp;</p>

					<div id="s-lg-box-21707184-container" class="s-lib-box-container">
						<div id="s-lg-box-21707184" class="s-lib-box s-lib-box-std">
							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Copyright duration
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-21707184" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-48895420" class="  clearfix">
				<p>In general, for works created on or after January 1, 1978, the term of copyright is the life of the author plus seventy years after the author&rsquo;s death. If the work is a joint work with multiple authors, the term lasts for seventy years after the last surviving author&rsquo;s death. For works made for hire and anonymous or pseudonymous works, the duration of copyright is 95 years from publication or 120 years from creation, whichever is shorter. For a chart detailing these and many&nbsp;other copyright terms, visit Cornell University Library&#39;s <a href="https://copyright.cornell.edu/publicdomain" target="_blank">Copyright Term and the Public Domain in the United States</a>.</p>

		   </div>
								</div>
								
							</div>
						</div>
					</div>
							</div>
							<div id="s-lg-box-wrapper-25465506" class="s-lg-box-wrapper-25465506">
					<div id="s-lg-box-21708005-container" class="s-lib-box-container">
						<div id="s-lg-box-21708005" class="s-lib-box s-lib-box-std">
							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Originality and fixation: conditions of copyright
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-21708005" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-48897290" class="  clearfix">
				<p>Copyright is a form of protection provided by the laws of the United States to the authors of &ldquo;original works of authorship&rdquo; that are fixed in a tangible form of expression. An original work of authorship is a work that is independently created by a human author and possesses at least some minimal degree of creativity. A work is &ldquo;fixed&rdquo; when it is captured (either by or under the authority of an author) in a sufficiently permanent medium such that the work can be perceived, reproduced, or communicated for more than a short time. Copyright protection in the United States exists automatically from the moment the original work of authorship is fixed.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><img alt="" loading="lazy" src="https://libapps.s3.amazonaws.com/accounts/76004/images/filmcamera.jpg" /></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><span class="photo_usage-attribution is-block" data-v-e1c1f65a="" id="attribution"><a data-v-e1c1f65a="" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/28853433@N02/12657103853" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Assaajuq regarde dans l&rsquo;objectif d&rsquo;une cam&eacute;ra...&quot;</a> <span data-v-e1c1f65a=""> by <a data-v-e1c1f65a="" href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/28853433@N02" rel="noopener" target="_blank">BiblioArchives / LibraryArchives</a></span> is licensed under <a class="photo_license" data-v-e1c1f65a="" href="https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.0/?ref=ccsearch&amp;atype=rich" rel="noopener" target="_blank"> CC BY 2.0 </a></span></p>

		   </div>
								</div>
								
							</div>
						</div>
					</div>
							</div>
							<div id="s-lg-box-wrapper-25464742" class="s-lg-box-wrapper-25464742">
					<div id="s-lg-box-21707341-container" class="s-lib-box-container">
						<div id="s-lg-box-21707341" class="s-lib-box s-lib-box-std">
							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Types of protected works
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-21707341" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-48895794" class="  clearfix">
				<p>Not all types of works are protected. Here are some that are:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Literary works</li>
	<li>Musical works, including any accompanying words</li>
	<li>Dramatic works, including any accompanying music</li>
	<li>Pantomimes and choreographic works</li>
	<li>Pictorial, graphic, and sculptural works</li>
	<li>Motion pictures and other audiovisual works</li>
	<li>Sound recordings</li>
	<li>Architectural works</li>
</ul>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
		   </div>
								</div>
								
							</div>
						</div>
					</div>
							</div>
							<div id="s-lg-box-wrapper-25464817" class="s-lg-box-wrapper-25464817">
					<div id="s-lg-box-21707407-container" class="s-lib-box-container">
						<div id="s-lg-box-21707407" class="s-lib-box s-lib-box-std">
							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">What is not protected by copyright?
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-21707407" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-48895848" class="  clearfix">
				<p>Copyright does not protect</p>

<ul>
	<li>Ideas, procedures, methods, systems, processes, concepts, principles, or discoveries</li>
	<li>Works that are not fixed in a tangible form (such as a choreographic work that has not been&nbsp;notated or recorded or an improvisational speech that has not been written down)</li>
	<li>Titles, names, short phrases, and slogans</li>
	<li>Familiar symbols or designs</li>
	<li>Mere variations of typographic ornamentation, lettering, or coloring</li>
	<li>Mere listings of ingredients or contents</li>
	<li>Most U.S. government works</li>
	<li>Works in the public domain</li>
</ul>
