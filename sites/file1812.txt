		<p><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Interested in getting a job in Marketing or Public Relations?</span></p>
<p><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">The Muscular Dystrophy Association has an exciting marketing/PR internship opportunity in its Allentown office.  In this position, you will responsible for organizing and implementing various projects to promote annual fundraising events, which raise over $1,100,000 annually. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small"> </span><span style="text-decoration: underline"><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Responsibilities include (but are not limited to):</span></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Researching and evaluating marketing/PR opportunities in various local markets</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Developing and implementing a marketing and public relations campaign including community newspapers, direct mail, internet, radio and TV</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Communicating with advertising representatives</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Help for secure auction items for upcoming events</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Writing articles, press releases and general communications promoting the event</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Additional projects as assigned</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline"><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Qualifications</span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">You must be a current college sophomore, junior or senior majoring in marketing, public relations or a related field with an interest in public or media relations.  Qualified candidates will possess the following skills:</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small"> </span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">An understanding of media and effective advertising methods</span></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Strong writing ability</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Excellent interpersonal skills</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Detail-oriented</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">An ability to work productively alone or as part of a team</span></li>
<li><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">Familiarity with MS Office</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">This is an unpaid internship designed for students who receive college credits. Days and hours are flexible.  </span></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small">The Muscular Dystrophy Association</span></strong><span style="font-family: Arial;font-size: x-small"> is a voluntary health agency — a dedicated partnership between scientists and concerned citizens aimed at conquering neuromuscular diseases that affect more than a million Americans.  MDA combats neuromuscular diseases through programs of worldwide research, comprehensive medical and community services, and far-reaching professional and public health education.  <span style="color: black">For additional information, visit <a title="blocked::http://www.mdausa.org/" href="http://www.mdausa.org/">www.mdausa.org</a></span></span></p>
