                    <div id="s-lg-content-33949181" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.proquest.com/eebo?accountid=40980"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '33949181'});">Early English Books Online</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-33949181" class="s-lg-database-desc">Digital facsimile page images of virtually every work printed in England, Ireland, Scotland, Wales and British North America and works in English printed elsewhere from 1473-1700.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-33949152" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.galegroup.com/itweb/mul_coll?db=ECCO"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '33949152'});">Eighteenth Century Collections Online (ECCO)</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-33949152" class="s-lg-database-desc">Over 180,000 titles (200,000 volumes) comprising books, pamphlets, essays, broadsides and more based on the English Short Title Catalogue Works published in the UK during the 18th century plus thousands from elsewhere. Content covers critical information in the fields of history, literature, religion, law, fine arts, science and more. Primarily in English but also includes other languages.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-36759095" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-2"><li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-33975981" class=""><span><a href="https://books.google.com" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '33975981'});">Google Books</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-33975981" class="s-lg-link-desc">Google Books is a rich source of full-text documents (books and other materials) published prior to 1923 that are now in the public domain.</div>
			</div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-36762071" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-10"><li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-41009267" class=""><a href="https://voices.revealdigital.org/"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '41009267'});">Independent Voices</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-41009267" class="s-lg-database-desc">"...a digital collection of alternative press newspapers, magazines and journals, drawn from the special collections of participating libraries. These periodicals were produced by feminists, dissident GIs, campus radicals, Native Americans, anti-war activists, Black Power advocates, Hispanics, LGBT activists, the extreme right-wing press and alternative literary magazines during the latter half of the 20th century."</div>
                    </div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-36763440" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-2"><li class="">

				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-33977663" class="s-lg-link-desc">Complete access to New York Times via Muhlenberg subscription. All users must register while physically on campus, using their Muhlenberg email.</div>
			</div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-36763441" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-10"><li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-36861598" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.proquest.com/hnpnewyorktimeswindex/advanced?accountid=40980"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '36861598'});">New York Times Archive, 1857-2015</a> 
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-36861598" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.proquest.com/hnpnewyorktimeswindex/advanced?accountid=40980"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '36861598'});">New York Times Archive, 1857-2015</a> 
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-46329902" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.alexanderstreet.com/ativ"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '46329902'});">Theatre in Video</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-46329902" class="s-lg-database-desc">Theatre in Video contains hundreds of videos, including documentaries and definitive performances of the world’s most important plays. From celebrated productions of Shakespeare to rare, in-depth footage of the work of Samuel Beckett, the collection covers a wide range of 20th century theatre history. Interviews with directors, designers, writers, and actors, along with excerpts of live performances deliver an authentic, behind-the-scenes look at hundreds of productions.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-33949190" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/mul_coll?db=TTDA"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '33949190'});">Times Digital Archive</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-33949190" class="s-lg-database-desc">Since its first publication in 1785, The Times (London) has become one of the most preeminent, global daily (except Sundays) newspapers of the 18th- through early 21st-centuries centuries, covering international events, people, places, politics, business news, opinion and debate, entertainment and advertising. The Times Digital Archive makes this highly regarded resource available in an easy-to-navigate, fully-searchable online resource containing every page of every issue within the range of years displayed in the product banner.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
