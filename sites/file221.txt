				<p>The digitized <em>Ciarla</em> is hosted by the Internet Archive&nbsp;and is arranged by academic year. It can be searched&nbsp;<span style="font-weight: 700;">by keyword</span>&nbsp;across the entire collection, or by&nbsp;selecting the desired academic year.</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 700;">Search the entire <i>Ciarla</i></span></p>

<p>Once you have reached the&nbsp;<a href="https://archive.org/details/theciarla?sort=-date" target="_blank">landing page</a>&nbsp;for the <i>Ciarlas</i>, which are sorted by date from most recent to oldest, you can enter a keyword in the search box on the right (see below). Be sure to select the button for &quot;Text contents&quot;, not&nbsp;&nbsp;&quot;Metadata.&quot; Once you have typed your keyword and selected &quot;Text contents,&quot; hit &quot;Enter&quot; on your keyboard (at this time, there is no &quot;Search&quot; button).</p>

<p><img alt="" src="//libapps.s3.amazonaws.com/accounts/76007/images/IA_wholeSearch_002.png" style="border-width: 1px; border-style: solid;" /></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>Once you have hit &quot;Enter,&quot; search results will appear at left, with relevant hits occurring in each issue shown in yellow (see below). Please note: the date order will now have been disrupted; you may again sort in date order by clicking the &quot;Date Archived&quot; sort text and changing it to &quot;Date Published.&quot;</p>

<p><img alt="" src="//libapps.s3.amazonaws.com/accounts/76007/images/IA_ciarla_highlights.png" style="border-width: 1px; border-style: solid;" /></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>&nbsp;You may then click on any issue/year of the Weekly and that year will open, with the search terms marked at the bottom of the screen with blue indicators (see below). Each indicator may be clicked to jump to the relevant page; the search term will be highlighted in purple. You may use the buttons in th lower&nbsp;right&nbsp;to flip pages, zoom in and out, and change the page layout.</p>

<p><span style="font-weight: 700;">Searching within&nbsp;one year of the&nbsp;<em>Ciarla</em></span></p>

<p>Once the&nbsp;<a href="https://archive.org/details/ciarla196800muhl/mode/2up" target="_blank">chosen year</a>&nbsp;has been selected, enter your search term in the&nbsp;<span style="font-weight: 700;"><em>lower</em></span>&nbsp;<span style="font-weight: 700;">search box with the text &quot;Search inside&quot;&nbsp;</span>(see below).&nbsp;Using the top white box will result in your leaving the&nbsp;<em>Weekly</em>&nbsp;and searching the entire Internet Archive. Once you have searched, the blue indicators will appear along the bottom, as discussed above.</p>
