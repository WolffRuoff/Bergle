<p>The keynote address was given by <a href="http://scrawford.net/blog/about/" target="_blank">Susan Crawford</a>, Professor of Cardoza Law School in New York City and Visiting Research Collaborator at Princeton&#8217;s <a href="http://citp.princeton.edu/" target="_blank">Center for Information Technology Policy</a>.</p>
<p>In her talk, Crawford outlined the virtual lock that local cable operators have established over high speed Internet service, endangering real competition and threatening the protection of net neutrality.  All of the talks at the conference are available for viewing online <a href="http://www.livestream.com/internetsocietychapters" target="_blank">here</a>.  Although it was a long day, the bus ride into New York was well worth it!</p>
