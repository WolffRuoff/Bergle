<p><strong><u>MDA/MTA Liason</u></strong></p>
<p>*In order to run for this position, you should be an active member of both the MDA and the MTA.</p>
<p>The MDA/MTA Liaison aids in facilitating the lines of communication between the MTA and MDA boards. They attend both board meetings and inform each board on what is happening in the other board. They attend both club meetings and keep both organizations updated with information on the other&#8217;s events. The liaison has to know who is in charge of different tasks within both organizations so they can guide people through the correct channels of communication. The liaison is responsible for being a resource to help students entering Muhlenberg that wish to join both organizations understand what it is like to do so. The liaison is also responsible for planning one event per semester for the MDA and MTA combined.</p>

<p><strong><u>Senior Class Representative:</u></strong></p>
<p>Senior Class Representative is in charge of planning one event each semester where both the MDA and Muhlenberg College Community can benefit. Sample events include guest speakers, improv jams, or photoshoots. Funds raised from the fall semester’s program go directly toward the senior showcase in the spring. The Senior Class Representative is also in charge of decorations and gifts for the Crew and Artistic Director for Moving Stories, Master Choreographers, and Dance Emerge.  The senior class representative is also in charge of coordinating the senior pub night with the MDA and MTA.</p>
<p>It is important that the class representative be organized, helpful, open-minded, and have good communication skills. <p><strong><u>Junior Class Representative:</u></strong></p>
<p>The class representatives of the MDA board are responsible for coming up with and putting together events and fundraisers that will help to raise money for the MDA. The events and fundraisers can be geared toward the MDA, but must be accessible to the entire campus. The events and fundraisers are created with the students of Muhlenberg in mind. Whatever it is that they would like to partake in or would like to learn about, the class rep tries to create something that will do so, all the while getting the name of the Muhlenberg Dance Association out to the students on campus and beyond. These events serve as a medium to advertise the Muhlenberg Dance Association to the rest of the campus.</p>
<p>The class representatives are also responsible for decorating the dressing rooms when a mainstage performance is being held, such as: Moving Stories, Masters, and Dance Emerge. The representatives also purchase gifts for the artistic directors of dance performances, as well as the wardrobe and stage crew.</p>

<p><strong><u>Freshman Class Representative</u></strong></p>
<p>*Note: this position is to be elected in the Fall semester at the second or third general MDA meeting.</p>
<p>The Freshman Class Representative of the MDA’s responsibility is to create a bridge between the Freshman class and the Muhlenberg Dance Association. Freshman often do not know what things are or where they are happening, so it is your job to keep the Freshman dancers engaged and informed of the MDA events, meetings, and opportunities around campus. In order to do this, you attend meetings with upperclassmen board members, plan events, and then reach out to the Freshman class. The freshman class rep is involved with designing apparel, decorating dressing rooms, and informing their fellow classmates on the rules and regulations of the department.  Freshman don’t understand all of the requirements, so it is the job of the freshman rep to relay that information to them.  It is a great opportunity to meet upperclassmen and get involved with dance at Muhlenberg!</p>
