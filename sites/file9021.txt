							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Primary vs. Secondary Sources
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-22790853" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-51653191" class="  clearfix">
				<p>A primary source in History is any text or artifact from the time period and/or place being discussed, or connecting the audience directly to the time or place being discussed (as in a personal interview or documentary).&nbsp;&nbsp;A secondary source, on the other hand, is authored by a scholar or other person analyzing the event after the fact, sometimes long afterward (as in a scholarly article or book).</p>
