			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2012/10/something-out-of-nothing/" rel="bookmark">Something Out of Nothing</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2012/10/something-out-of-nothing/" title="9:20 am" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">October 31, 2012</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/db240243/" title="View all posts by Dani">Dani</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>Over the semester, I have been working on a theatre production in the blackbox season. Blackboxes are the next set of productions after the Mainstage shows. They are completely produced by students, and each show is given a small budget to put on a production in the Studio Theatre on campus. It is a great opportunity for people to explore what the theatre can be and use the skills they have learned in the classroom in a actual production.</p>
<p>The production I am working on is called &#8220;A La Luz Del Miedo,&#8221; translated as &#8220;In Light of Fear.&#8221; It is a piece that at the beginning of the process was just a concept based off of a painting with no script. Now, with the show coming up, we have a script that was written completely by the director, actors and stage managers. It has been an amazing process that has shown me just how much can be done in the theatre and the ability the theatre has to push the boundaries. We have spent the rehearsal process working in all kinds of venues and mediums.</p>
<div id="attachment_1390" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/luz-in-the-park-.jpeg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-1390" class="size-medium wp-image-1390" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/luz-in-the-park--300x225.jpeg" alt="" width="300" height="225" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/luz-in-the-park--300x225.jpeg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/luz-in-the-park-.jpeg 720w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-1390" class="wp-caption-text">Rehearsal in the park</p></div>
<div id="attachment_1391" style="width: 235px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/luz-park-2-.jpeg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-1391" class="size-medium wp-image-1391" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/luz-park-2--225x300.jpeg" alt="" width="225" height="300" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/luz-park-2--225x300.jpeg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/luz-park-2-.jpeg 540w" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-1391" class="wp-caption-text">More rehearsal in the park</p></div>
<div id="attachment_1389" style="width: 235px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/a-la-luz-monster.jpeg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-1389" class=" wp-image-1389" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/a-la-luz-monster-225x300.jpeg" alt="" width="225" height="300" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/a-la-luz-monster-225x300.jpeg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2012/10/a-la-luz-monster.jpeg 720w" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-1389" class="wp-caption-text">Creation of a monster with bodies</p></div>
<p>As you can see, a lot of the things that we have done are a little out there, but that is the beauty of it. It is great that there are so many opportunities here to explore theatre, and I am happy to be working on a production in a way that I never have before. I have learned a lot and can take a very unique experience away from it that I will have forever.</p>
