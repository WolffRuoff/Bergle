<div id="primary">
        <p><p><span><span>The Lehigh Valley LGBT Community Archive, a partnership between the Bradbury-Sullivan LGBT Community Center and Trexler Library, Muhlenberg College, is excited to share an initial collection of oral histories from members of the Lehigh Valley LGBT community. Our first collection, "<a href="http://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/lgbt_oralhistory/collections/show/1">Stories from LGBT Older Adults in the Lehigh Valley</a>," is the result of a collaboration among faculty and staff from area institutions along with partners from within the community. </span></span></p>
<p>We are also in the process of completing oral histories collected during the COVID-19 pandemic, in which members of the community discuss the current public health crisis as it affects LGBTQ+ individuals, as well as reflecting on experiences of the HIV/AIDS crisis in the 1980s and '90s. <br /><br />Both of these initiatives have been s<span>ponsored in part by the Lehigh Valley Engaged Humanities Consortium, </span><span>with generous support provided by a grant to Lafayette College from the Andrew W. </span><span>Mellon Foundation.<br /></span><br />This site utilizes the Oral History Metadata Synchronizer <a href="https://www.oralhistoryonline.org/">(OHMS)</a>, an open-source application developed by the Louie B. Nunn Center for Oral History at the University of Kentucky Libraries, which allows word-level search capability within time-stamped transcriptions, and facilitates indexing and the addition of keywords.<span><br /></span></p></p>
