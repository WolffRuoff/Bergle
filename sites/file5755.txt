The Shorewomen are off to a 4-0 start in CC play, while the Mules are 2-0.</p>
<p>Live stats Game 1: <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu">http://www.muhlenberg.edu/sports/softlive/xlive.htm</a></p>
<p>Live stats Game 2: <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu">http://www.muhlenberg.edu/sports/softlive2/xlive.htm</a></p>
<p>Gettysburg did not make the CC playoffs last year but is traditionally one of the strongest teams in the league, and one that gives Muhlenberg a hard time. The Mules have never swept the Bullets in a regular-season doubleheader, and they haven&#8217;t won a game at Gettysburg since 2000.</p>
<p>Live stats: <a href="http://client.stretchinternet.com/client/gettysburg.portal">http://client.stretchinternet.com/client/gettysburg.portal</a>#</p>
<p><strong>Baseball vs. Ursinus (Friday, 3:30); at McDaniel (Saturday, 12:30 doubleheader); at Centenary (Sunday, 12:00)</strong></p>
<p>The 16-6 Mules, who received 11 votes in this week&#8217;s D3baseball.com poll, look to continue their winning ways with three CC games and one non-league contest. Coming off Tuesday&#8217;s 11-5 win at Ursinus, Muhlenberg will try to complete its first season sweep of the Bears on Friday.</p>
<p>Live stats: <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu">http://www.muhlenberg.edu/sports/baselive/xlive.htm</a></p>
<p>Saturday&#8217;s twinbill is against a McDaniel team that, like Muhlenberg, has shown great improvement: The Green Terror is 11-4 after winning 12 games all of last season. The Mules, who have scored in double figures in 13 of their 22 games, will try to solve a McDaniel pitching staff that has a sparkling 2.40 ERA.</p>
<p>Live stats: <a href="http://www.mcdanielathletics.com/livestats/landing">http://www.mcdanielathletics.com/livestats/landing</a></p>
<p>Sunday&#8217;s non-conference matchup will be played at TD Bank Ballpark in Bridgewater, N.J., the home of the Somerset Patriots of the Atlantic League.</p>
<p>Live stats: <a href="http://dl.dropbox.com/u/52657926/Baseball/xhome.htm">http://dl.dropbox.com/u/52657926/Baseball/xhome.htm</a></p>
<p><strong>Lacrosse doubleheader vs. Washington (Saturday, men at 1:00, women at 4:00)</strong></p>
<p>Both Mule lax teams are seeking their first CC win of the season. Both lost by three at Washington last year but beat the Shoremen/women on their last trip to Allentown.</p>
<p>Live stats (men): <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu">http://www.muhlenberg.edu/sports/mlaxlive/xlive.htm</a></p>
<p>Live stats (women): <a href="http://www.muhlenberg.edu">http://www.muhlenberg.edu/sports/wlaxlive/xlive.htm</a></p>
<p>Live video (both): <a href="http://www.livestream.com/muhlenberg">http://www.livestream.com/muhlenberg</a></p>
<p><strong>Tennis doubleheader vs. Washington (Saturday, men at 11:00, women at 3:00)</strong></p>
<p>After evening their CC mark at 2-2 with a dramatic win vs. Franklin &amp; Marshall last night, the Mule women look to make it two in a row against a Washington team that reached the CC championship match last year. The Mule men are looking for their first win of the spring.</p>
<p><strong>Men&#8217;s Golf at McDaniel Spring Invitational (Saturday and Sunday)</strong></p>
<p>The Mules compete in one of their biggest tournaments of the season, with 18 teams scheduled to tee off in the two-day event. Among their opponents will be five other CC teams.</p>
<p>Live stats: <a href="http://www.mcdanielathletics.com/sports/mgolf/live-stats">http://www.mcdanielathletics.com/sports/mgolf/live-stats</a></p>
<p><strong>Track &amp; Field at Lafayette 7-Way Meet (Saturday)</strong></p>
<p>At their first outdoor meet of the season, the Mules will be the only Division III squad. The other teams scheduled to compete are Division I Lafayette, Lehigh, St. Joseph&#8217;s, Rider and Monmouth and Division II East Stroudsburg.</p>
