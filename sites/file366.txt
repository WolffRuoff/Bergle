            <section>
                <h2>Digital Projects</h2>

                <p>Materials from Special Collections and Archives have been incorporated into digital projects across campus, many of which were created by students as part of an independent study or internship. Others were the result of collaborations between Special Collections and departments across campus. A sampling of these projects is made available here.</p>
            </section>


            <section class="d-projects">
						
								<div class="dp" id="d-band">
                    <div class="dp-img">
                        <img src="/library/media/contentassets/library/images/special-collections/otherpagepictures/AllentownBand_logo_square.png" alt="View Allentown Band Archives">
                    </div>
                    <div class="dp-txt">
                        <a href="https://www.allentownband.trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/" target="_blank"><h3 class="dp-h">Allentown Band Archives and Storytelling Partnership</h3></a> 												      
                        <p class="dp-p">Since the summer of 2018, Special Collections and Archives has partnered with the historic Allentown Band to help catalog its resources and make them more accessible to the public.  This project site is the result of that work, which has taken place through coursework, internships, and independent studies. The Band's historical image collection can be accessed through the site. (<a href="https://www.muhlenberg.edu/news/more-than-music/" target="_blank">Read more</a>).</p>

                    </div>
                </div>
								
                <div class="dp" id="d-ptop">
                    <div class="dp-img">
                        <img src="/library/media/contentassets/library/images/special-collections/otherpagepictures/pathways.jpg" alt="View Pathways to Present Application">
                    </div>
                    <div class="dp-txt">
                        <a href="http://pathways.muhlenberg.edu/" target="_blank"><h3 class="dp-h">“Muhlenberg Pathways to Present” walking tour of campus</h3></a> 												      
                        <p class="dp-p">Muhlenberg Pathways to Present is a website and free app that put the history of Muhlenberg College at your fingertips. Take a self-guided walking tour of the campus to learn more about the individuals, buildings, and events that shaped the College. Explore its history though archival materials, interviews, and student research.</p>

                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="dp" id="d-vis-tl">
                    <div class="dp-img">
                        <img src="/library/media/contentassets/library/images/special-collections/otherpagepictures/Eleanor.jpg" alt="View Muhlenberg Visitors Timeline">
                    </div>
                    <div class="dp-txt">
                        <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/specialcollections/digitalprojects/muhlenberg-visitors-timeline/" target="_blank"><h3 class="dp-h">Muhlenberg College Timeline of Visitors</h3></a>

                        <p class="dp-p">Maddux Pearson '18 dug into the College's archives to create an interactive timeline of the most significant visitors to come to campus in the 20th century. (<a href="https://www.muhlenberg.edu/news/2018/muhlenbergsdistinguishedguests.html" target="_blank">Read more</a>).</p>
                    </div>    
                </div>

                <div class="dp" id="d-thtr-tl">
                    <div class="dp-img">
                        <img src="/library/media/contentassets/library/images/special-collections/otherpagepictures/Theatre.jpg" alt="View Muhlenberg Theatre History Timeline">
                    </div>
                    <div class="dp-txt">
                        <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/specialcollections/digitalprojects/theatrehistorytimeline/" target="_blank">
                            <h3 class="dp-h">Muhlenberg's Theatre History Timeline</h3></a>														
                        <p class="dp-p">Researching in the archives, Sasha Cheer '19 crafted a timeline of theatre history at Muhlenberg from its origins in the 19th century through today.</p>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="dp" id="d-feather-map">
                    <div class="dp-img">
                        <img src="/library/media/contentassets/library/images/special-collections/otherpagepictures/Fetherolf.jpg" alt="View the Fetherolf Story Map">
                    </div>
                    <div class="dp-txt">
                        <a href="https://uploads.knightlab.com/storymapjs/feea6db3e8e40b07e7a1e0f7dc0feac6/martin-fetherolf-in-france-1918-1919/index.html" target="_blank">
                            <h3 class="dp-h">World War I in France through the eyes of Martin D. Fetherolf, Class of 1914</h3>
                        </a>

                        <p class="dp-p">Jona Lieberman ‘20 analyzed the manuscript of Martin D. Fetherolf’s war memoir to create a story-map of his experiences. (<a href="https://www.muhlenberg.edu/news/2019/trackingalieutenantstravels.html" target="_blank">Read more</a>).</p>

                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="dp" id="d-mmp">
                    <div class="dp-img">
                        <img src="/library/media/contentassets/library/images/special-collections/otherpagepictures/MMP.jpg" alt="View The Muhlenberg Memories Project">
                    </div>
                    <div class="dp-txt">
                        <a href="http://muhlenbergmemoriesproject.com/" target="_blank">
                            <h3 class="dp-h">The Muhlenberg Memories Project</h3>
                        </a>

                        <p class="dp-p">Explore Muhlenberg in the 1940s and 1960s, learn about its historic buildings, and more through this multimodal platform create by Dr. Kate Ranieri and Anthony Dalton of Media & Communication, in collaboration with Special Collections. (<a href="http://muhlenbergmemoriesproject.com/" target="_blank">Read more</a>).
