								<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p>So we went to our project adviser&#8217;s house for dinner on Tuesday and it was amazing! Not only does his house look absolutely fantastic but the food, oh my god, delicious. I am not going to lie, I was a little skeptical at first because he is a vegetarian so I knew whatever we ate it wouldn&#8217;t have meat in it and I WAS convinced that a meal wasn&#8217;t complete unless it had meat in it and let me just say I like my meat, so I was a little concerned. However, as we ate dinner, I was happily mistaken. We had vegetarian &#8220;chicken&#8221; and it was actually really good. It was meat but not really but that didn&#8217;t matter because it was good. I even ate seconds. There were also these great beans that were just so good, I could probably just right a blog post specifically on how good those beans were but i&#8217;m not so just take my word on it. After dinner we at<span style="line-height: 1.714285714;font-size: 1rem">e dessert, yes there was dessert, and boy was it good. I really wish I took a picture so you could see it but I was too busy a</span><span style="line-height: 1.714285714;font-size: 1rem">dmiring it myself. There was this one that had a layer of brownie on the top and chocolat</span><span style="line-height: 1.714285714;font-size: 1rem">e pudding on the bottom. We ate it with vanilla ice cream and it was still warm from being in the oven, ugh it was so good. The other was this strawberry short cake that had chunks of real strawberry in it, it was great. We all talked about how great the food was and about things we liked. Some people told funny stories about their parents and grandparents and technology, it was funny,</span></p>
<p>Because our project adviser also has a pool because he is aweso<span style="line-height: 1.714285714;font-size: 1rem">me, some of us decided to go swimming! It was really a good time. I didn&#8217;t go in the pool but I splashed water at </span><span style="line-height: 1.714285714;font-size: 1rem">the people who were and watched them try to create a whirlpool by swimming in a big circle. It looked like a witch&#8217;s cauldron, all I was missing was a huge stirrer. It was so relaxing, I just wish I had a bathing suit! Don&#8217;t forget your bathing suits, we have a great pool in the Life&#8217;s sports center that you are going to want to use. I can&#8217;t wait to go back to their house and actually get in the pool. This was definitely a great day!</span></p>
