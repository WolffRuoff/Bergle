Otherwise, your material will only be available for in-library research per the </span><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Roboto, sans-serif; background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"><font color="#1155cc"><u><a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/specialcollections/about/" target="_blank">Special Collections Access Policy</a>.</u></font></span></span></p>
