	<h1 class="entry-title">Joint Steering/Selection Committee Meeting</h1>

	<div class="entry-meta">
		<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://mcerp.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2016/02/29/joint-steeringselection-committee-meeting/" title="10:41 am" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">February 29, 2016</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://mcerp.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/" title="View all posts by "></a></span>	</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<p>ORACLE IS NO LONGER A CANDIDATE</p>
<p>The first meeting between Steering and Selection was indeed productive.  As part of the process, the Steering and Selection Committees voted to remove Oracle as a vendor choice.  It was the committees&#8217; opinion that Oracle, despite it&#8217;s maturity as a company, does not have a mature product in the liberal arts college space.</p>
<p>Please disregard any communications you may have seen in the past about Oracle for the ERP Selection Process.  If you see something that references Oracle, feel free to contact me, John Glasgow.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
