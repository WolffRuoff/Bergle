								<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p><a href="http://mediacom.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2011/03/PPRS.png"><img class="size-full wp-image-577 alignright" src="http://mediacom.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2011/03/PPRS.png" alt="" width="163" height="123" /></a>The Pennsylvania Public Relations Society (PPRS) is offering a $1,000 scholarship opportunity to full-time Pennsylvania college students majoring in a communications-related field of study. The application deadline in May 6, 2011.</p>
<p>Download the application here: <a href="http://mediacom.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2011/03/PPRS_Scholarship_App_2011.pdf">PPRS_Scholarship_App_2011</a></p>
<p>The criteria and requirements are as follows:</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Selection Criteria</strong></p>
<p>One $1,000.00 scholarship recipient will be selected based on the following criteria:</p>
<p>1.   A legal Pennsylvania resident</p>
<p>2.   A full-time student in their junior or senior year in Fall 2011 and majoring in a communications-related discipline at a Pennsylvania college or university</p>
<p>3.   Academic achievement (must have a current college grade point average of 2.75 or higher, based on a 4.0 scale)</p>
<p>4.   Extracurricular activities and campus involvement</p>
<p>5.   Strong writing skills</p>
<p><span id="more-575"></span></p>
<p><strong>Application Requirements</strong></p>
<p>Please submit the following, postmarked by <strong>May 6, 2011</strong>, in order to be considered for the scholarship:</p>
<p>1.   A completed application form, including Personal Essay, on a separate sheet of paper.</p>
<p>2.   Official college/university transcript with most recent semester grades</p>
<p>3.   Two letters of recommendation, at least one of which must come from a communications professor from your respective school.</p>
<p>4.   Materials should be submitted together in one envelope.</p>
<p><strong> </strong></p>
<p><strong>Return the completed application to:</strong></p>
<p>Justin Fleming<br />
PPRS Student Development Committee Chair<br />
C/O NASW-PA<br />
425 N. 21<sup>st</sup> Street<br />
Suite 401<br />
Camp Hill, PA 17011-2223</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline">The completed application must be postmarked on or before Friday, May 6, 2011</span></strong><strong>. </strong>A committee of PPRS members will review all applications. Applicants will be notified regarding selection results. The scholarship winner will be invited to and recognized at an upcoming PPRS meeting. The winner may choose to have the $1,000 award made out to his or her university or university bookstore. A recipient may apply for the scholarship for three consecutive semesters provided the student maintains at least a 2.75 GPA in his or her major and overall. PPRS does not discriminate on the basis of age, race, creed, color, gender, religion, nationality, disability, lifestyle or sexual orientation. PPRS reserves the right not to award a scholarship in any given year without recourse. The decision of PPRS is final.</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><em>All information submitted to the PPRS Student Development Committee will remain confidential</em></p>
