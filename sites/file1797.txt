		<p><a href="https://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/current-internship-opportunities/communication/">Alliance for Sustainable Communities: Communication Internship</a></p>
<p>Communicating sustainability concepts to the community at large is a challenge, especially considering the flood of misinformation in the mainstream media. Effective communication can be quite challenging, because we often need to make scientific concepts, technical data, and moral &amp; ethical &amp; moral issues clear and engaging to the public.</p>
<p>While it is difficult to spell out in the specific activities &amp; topics months in advance, it is safe to say that interns will likely work on print, online, and in-person communications, including reports, posters, presentations, web pages, and/or news releases — and this may at times include controversial issues.</p>
<p>One challenging ‘umbrella’ topic is the extent to which media &amp; communication reinforce the unsustainable practices that are causing so many of our problems, instead of helping move to a more sustainable future. Examples of likely focus areas for 2019 include the following, all of which have technical, social-justice, and community engagement aspects::</p>
<ul>
<li>Community planning &amp; governance that often seems to benefit developers at the expense of the community</li>
<li>Education (Pre-K–12 and higher ed) that has failed to make young people ‘sustainability literate’</li>
<li>Justice systems that exacerbate problems of inequality and injustice</li>
<li>‘Business as usual’ approaches that perpetuate problems.</li>
</ul>
<p>Specific activities depend in part on what is happening in the community during the internship</p>
<p>Note: This internship is usually also available with a graphic design focus for qualified students.</p>
<p><em><strong>?</strong></em> — Send an email to internship coordinator <a href="mailto:peter@sustainlv.org">Peter Crownfield</a></p>

<p><a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/current-internship-opportunities/editing-publishing/">Editing and Publishing internship Spring 2020</a></p>
<p>Help publish the 2020 edition of the Alliance’s annual Sustainable Lehigh Valley booklet. Update content, help select &amp; edit essays and other written material, and learn to lay out the final publication using InDesign professional publishing software. [No previous InDesign experience needed.]

<li>Maintain schedules and other planning to ensure on-time completion</li>
<li>Lay out and copyedit the publication using InDesign professional publishing software</li>

	    <h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/?p=1544" rel="bookmark" title="Climate &amp; Sustainability Education Internship">Climate &amp; Sustainability Education Internship</a></h2>
	    
	    <div class="post-meta">

			<span class="date-sep"> / </span>

	<div class="post-content">
		    		            			            	                                                                                            
		<p>Help Create a Just &amp; Sustainable world!</p>
<p>Rethink the System, Work for Change</p>
<p>Do you want to use your talents, insight, and academic knowledge to help create a better world? Is an Alliance internship right for you?</p>
<p>“The most important thing I did during my internship was to meet people and find out how many factors go into changing the way something is done.…  I learned how to find out about things that tend to be hidden, talk to people about things they would rather pretend do not exist, and solve problems that seem overwhelming at first glance.” (JT, Lafayette College)</p>
<p>“Working with the Alliance allowed me to collaborate with individuals who shared my passion for sustainability, as well as voice my own ideas…. a truly rewarding and enjoyable experience…” (LB, Muhlenberg College)</p>
<p>2016–2017</p>
<p>Alliance internships blend research, analysis, communication, and activism to raise both public &amp; professional awareness. If you are interested in one of the possibilities listed below, contact Peter Crownfield as soon as possible. (Before considering specific internships we suggest you read this <a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/">brief description</a>.)</p>
<p>Following is a list of internships we are featuring this year. We will not be able to do them all at the same time, of course, but all are available at this time.</p>
<p>▪ Climate &amp; Sustainability for Teachers – Help develop ideas and tools to help teachers integrate climate &amp; sustainability concepts in all subject areas and grade levels — and use them to generate critical thinking &amp; dialogue that can result in a major increase in understanding in the K-12 school population. <a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/current-internship-opportunities/climate-sustainability-for-schools/">[More info]</a></p>
<p>▪ NEW Communication–Climate &amp; Sustainability for Teachers Project – This internship involves a blend of research, keeping up with developments in the project, and communicating with teachers via email and a new blog. Some of this work can be done off-site, once the intern is familiar with the project and its goals. [see preceding internship for more information about the project] <a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/current-internship-opportunities/communication-%E2%80%93-climate-sustainability-for-teachers-project/">[More info]</a></p>
<p>▪ NEW Communication–Combined Heat &amp; Power [CHP] Project – This internship involves a blend of research, keeping up with developments in the project, and helping to prepare an effective proposal and presentation to management. <a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/current-internship-opportunities/combined-heat-power-chp/">[More info]</a></p>
<p>▪ NEW Sustainable Events! – Moving towards zero-waste, climate-neutral events. Will include working with specific events to make them more sustainable, probably including one on-campus event and one community-based event.</p>
<p>▪ Communication – Communicating sustainability concepts to the community at large is a challenge, given the flood of misinformation in the mainstream media. This can be quite challenging, because these projects focus on public policy but need to make scientific concepts, technical data, and moral &amp; ethical &amp; moral issues clear and engaging to the public. <a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/current-internship-opportunities/communication/">[More info]</a></p>
<p>Many of our internships were developed many in response to student requests, so please contact us if you are interested in an internship that’s not listed.</p>
<p>To apply: contact Peter Crownfield peter@sustainlv.org</p>
<p>Sustainability &amp; Health Initiative</p>
<p>Alliance for Sustainable Communities-Lehigh Valley</p>
<p>Bethlehem, PA 18015</p>
<p><a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/">http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/</a></p>
