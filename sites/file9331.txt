			<div id="s-lg-content-41239210" class="  clearfix">
				<p>Open Educational<span style="font-size:12px;"> Resource</span>s, or &quot;OER&quot; are defined as &quot;<span style="box-sizing: inherit;">teaching, learning, and research materials that are either (a) in the public domain or (b)</span>&nbsp;licensed<span style="box-sizing: inherit;">&nbsp;in a manner that provides everyone with free and perpetual permission to engage in the &quot;5R activities&quot;.</span></p>

<p>The 5R activities are:</p>

<p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; list-style-image: url(&quot;../images/bulletin.png&quot;); margin-left: 40px;"><span style="font-size:12px;"><span class="highlight" style="box-sizing: inherit;">Retain</span><span style="box-sizing: inherit;">&nbsp;</span>&ndash; make, own, and control a copy of the resource</span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; list-style-image: url(&quot;../images/bulletin.png&quot;); margin-left: 40px;"><span style="font-size:12px;"><span class="highlight" style="box-sizing: inherit;">Reuse</span><span style="box-sizing: inherit;">&nbsp;</span>&ndash; use your original, revised, or remixed copy of the resource publicly</span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; list-style-image: url(&quot;../images/bulletin.png&quot;); margin-left: 40px;"><span style="font-size:12px;"><span class="highlight" style="box-sizing: inherit;">Revise</span><span style="box-sizing: inherit;">&nbsp;</span>&ndash; edit, adapt, and modify your copy of the resource</span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; list-style-image: url(&quot;../images/bulletin.png&quot;); margin-left: 40px;"><span style="font-size:12px;"><span class="highlight" style="box-sizing: inherit;">Remix</span><span style="box-sizing: inherit;">&nbsp;</span>&ndash; combine your original or revised copy of the resource with other existing material to create something new</span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; list-style-image: url(&quot;../images/bulletin.png&quot;); margin-left: 40px;"><span style="font-size:12px;"><span class="highlight" style="box-sizing: inherit;">Redistribute</span><span style="box-sizing: inherit;">&nbsp;</span>&ndash; share copies of your original, revised, or remixed copy of the resource with others</span></p>

<p style="box-sizing: inherit; margin-bottom: 0.5em; list-style-image: url(&quot;../images/bulletin.png&quot;);">(from the <a href="https://creativecommons.org/about/program-areas/education-oer/">Creative Commons website</a>)</p>

<p>These textbooks&nbsp;are not necessarily in the public domain, but are freely available to download and use (just cite!).&nbsp; Most of the time their license allows educators to share and remix content, but check each text individually to be sure.</p>

			<div id="s-lg-content-41239315" class=""><span><a href="https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/online-textbooks/" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '41239315'});">MIT Open Courseware</a>
