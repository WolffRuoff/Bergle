<p>The staff of Trexler Library is pleased to support the 2019-2020 Center for Ethics program, <em>Borders, Nationalisms, Identities: The Ethics of Global Citizenship. </em>Kelly Cannon, Outreach and Scholarly Communication Librarian,<em> </em>researched and compiled a list of readings and videos to support this year’s Center for Ethics programming.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Selections include readings about and by featured fall speakers <strong>Ronny Quevedo, Theresa Cardinal Brown, Ieva Jusionyte, Amy-Jill Levine, Skye Fitzgerald </strong>as well as spring speaker <strong>Mohsin Hamid. </strong>The Center for Ethics is also sponsoring a trip to <strong>Ellis Island, </strong>and the guide features readings and a video on this gateway to America for 12 million immigrants.&nbsp;</p>



<p>The reading and video list can be found at: <a rel="noreferrer noopener" aria-label="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1UvpYmyKSpuuHQ0-xevLh7uL5RX7Eqxakrf0AlpGrmp0/edit?usp=sharing (opens in a new tab)" href="http://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/cfereadings20192020" target="_blank">http://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/cfereadings20192020</a></p>



<p>Please contact Trexler Library with any questions about this material at (484) 664-3600 or <a href="mailto:refdeskmailbox@muhlenberg.edu">refdeskmailbox@muhlenberg.edu</a>.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Are you interested in learning more about the next topic in the Center for Ethics (CFE) event series,  <br> <strong>“Standing on Sacred Ground”</strong>? Here is a list of readings and a movie, compiled from library resources and the web, to aid in understanding respect for the sacred lands of indigenous communities. The topic will be addressed by filmmaker Christopher McLeod and  project collaborator and Winnemem Wintu Tribal Chief, Caleen Sisk  at the next CFE event on February 26th.</p>



<h2>Christopher McLeod &#8211; Readings</h2>



<p><a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/oclc/51609102" target="_blank"><em>In the Light of Reverence</em></a>. Directed by Christopher McLeod, Jessica Abbe, Peter Coyote, Tantoo Cardinal, Vine Deloria, William Perry Pendley, Deb Liggett, Charles F Wilkinson, Lee Wayne Lomayestewa, Vernon Masayesva, Janet R Balsom, Kathy Hammond, Sharon Heywood, Rowena Pattee Kryder, Caleen Sisk-Franco, Independent Television Service, Native American Public Telecommunications, Inc, Sacred Land Film Project, Bullfrog Films, and Bullfrog Films, Inc. Bullfrog Films, 2002. DVD.<br></p>



<p>McLeod, Christopher (Toby). <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="http://sacredland.org/blog/" target="_blank"><em>Standing on Sacred Ground Director’s Blog</em></a>. 2018.<br></p>
