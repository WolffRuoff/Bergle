							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Activating the NYTimes.com Pass
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-15572440" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-33972308" class="  clearfix">
				<p><img alt="New York Times" height="73" loading="lazy" src="https://lgimages.s3.amazonaws.com/data/imagemanager/63912/the_new_york_times_logo.png" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" width="496" /></p>

<p>Trexler Library is happy to offer a NYTimes.com Academic Site License providing full access to news, multimedia, and archives of the New York Times. &nbsp;This access is only available to current Muhlenberg College faculty, students, and staff.</p>

<h2><b>To ACTIVATE your PASS:</b></h2>

<ul>
	<li><a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1xBMnlbKyBz-UwcSNEYOFByaKjTtA5T_EzGRhUlU135k/edit?usp=sharing" target="_blank">Step-by-step visual instructions available in this Google Doc</a> or use basic steps below:&nbsp;</li>
	<li>While on campus or connected to our campus <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/10q45D3a_Bd2xJ-ZyfvFJho_Lg9S8ac3zHtGVWOfB0Bk/edit?usp=sharing" target="_blank">VPN</a>, go to <a href="http://www.nytimes.com/grouppass">http://www.nytimes.com/grouppass</a>. If you are off-campus and do not have VPN access, please use this link: <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.nytimes.com/grouppass">https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.nytimes.com/grouppass</a></li>
	<li>Using your <b>Muhlenberg email address</b>, click on &lsquo;Create an Account&rsquo; or &lsquo;Log in to Continue&rsquo; if you already have an account.</li>
	<li>If activated correctly, you should see a screen indicating the expiration of your pass (should be about a year later).</li>
	<li>Once activated, you may now access NYTimes.com from any device, from any location (using your email address as the username and the password you created for this NYTimes pass).</li>
</ul>

<h2><b>Problems? </b></h2>

<ul>
	<li>The Group Pass must be activated while physically within our campus network, either on campus or VPN. If you are off-campus and do not have VPN access, please use this link: <a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.nytimes.com/grouppass">https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.nytimes.com/grouppass.</a></li>
	<li>After activation, you must be logged in to your NYTimes.com account which is associated with your Group Pass registration (Muhlenberg email address)</li>
	<li>If you are a current NYTimes Digital subscriber using your Muhlenberg email, you will not be able to claim a pass.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>If you have a daily pass, that pass must expire before you can claim this year-long&nbsp;pass.</li>
	<li>Other issues, contact Tina Hertel (<a href="mailto:thertel@muhlenberg.edu">tinahertel@muhlenberg.edu</a>) or x3550.</li>
</ul>

<blockquote>
<p><strong><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: 'NYTFranklin';"><em>At this time, access to articles from 1923 to 1980 is limited to five articles for the entire duration of your Pass.</em> </span></strong></p>
</blockquote>
