          <div class="abstract single_note"><span class='inline-label'>Scope and Contents</span>
	     This collection contains eight twentieth-century scrapbooks compiled by members of the Allentown Band. </h3>


<div class="badge-and-identifier">

     <div class="recordsummary" style="clear:both">

          <div class="abstract single_note"><span class='inline-label'>Scope and Contents</span>
	     This collection of fifteen binders has served the Band in a reference and preservation capacity. Some are dedicated to the Band's three longest-serving directors, Martin Klingler, Bert Meyers, and Ron Demkee. Others detail member-specific information, including member lists, instrument inventories, and death notices.The binders include newspaper clippings, correspondence, programs, published accounts of the Band and its history, and photographs.
