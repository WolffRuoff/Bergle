<p>On February 21, 2019, Muhlenberg College hosted a public discussion about the issue of legislative redistricting and the fairness, or unfairness, of legislative district maps in Pennsylvania. Trexler Library social sciences librarian, Jess Denke, has compiled a bibliography of recent articles and documents to provide further background on the issue. All of the articles and documents listed below are freely available to anyone (no need to be affiliated with Muhlenberg College). While the list is in alphabetical order by author, if you read the articles/documents in order of date published, starting with the League of Women Voters&#8217; January 2018 lawsuit, you will see a chain of actions and information emerge.</p>
