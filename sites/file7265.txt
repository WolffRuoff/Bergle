			<div id="s-lg-content-33974315" class="  clearfix">
				<p></p>
<p>Steps: <img src="http://lgimages.s3.amazonaws.com/data/imagemanager/63912/obshowto.jpg" alt="One Button Studio Quick Guide" /></p>
<ol>
<li>You will need a USB flash drive to use the One Button Studio. </li>
<li>Set up your presentation, connect your laptop to the projector if you want to project while presenting. </li>
<li>Set up the podium or area that you will be presenting. A podium is available to have your notes and remotes nearby. </li>
<li>Insert your flash drive into the port. This will initialize the one button system.</li>
<li>The room lights will dim and the studio lights will turn on. </li>
<li>The computer screen will tell you when the system is ready to begin. </li>
<li>Push the large silver button to begin your recording session. </li>
<li>Record your presentation, interview, portfolio, or whatever you are working on. </li>
<li>When you are done, push the large silver button again to end your recording session.</li>
<li>The system will then download the video file to your flashdrive. Please WAIT before removing your flashdrive from the system.</li>
<li>The studio lights will turn off and the regular room lights will turn back on. </li>
<li>You may now view your video on your own or edit it as needed. </li>
</ol>
