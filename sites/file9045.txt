                        <li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-56498954" class=""><span><a href="http://books.google.com" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '56498954'});">Google Books</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-56498954" class="s-lg-link-desc">An excellent source for full-text of primary documents as they appear in public domain (pre-1923) books and magazines.</div>
			</div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-56498982" class=""><span><a href="https://www.hathitrust.org/" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '56498982'});">Hathi Trust</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-56498982" class="s-lg-link-desc">Thousands of older books and journals, useful as primary documents, scanned by libraries throughout the U.S.</div>
			</div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-56567084" class=""><span><a href="https://muse-jhu-edu.muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/book/12897" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '56567084'});">Jim Crow: A Documentary History</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-56567084" class="s-lg-link-desc">A wealth of primary documents related to Jim Crow.</div>
			</div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-58975873" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-10"><li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-36861598" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.proquest.com/hnpnewyorktimeswindex/advanced?accountid=40980"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '36861598'});">New York Times Archive, 1857-2015</a> 
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-49546134" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://nwld.alexanderstreet.com"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '49546134'});">North American Women's Letters and Diaries</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-49546134" class="s-lg-database-desc">Colonial to 1950. Includes the immediate experiences of 1,325 women and 150,000 pages of diaries and letters.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-46336242" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.proquest.com/?accountid=40980&selectids=1007272,1009159,1005685,1009157,1007154,1006359"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '46336242'});">ProQuest Historical Newspapers</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-46336242" class="s-lg-database-desc">Includes full text of the following publications:<br />
Chicago Defender (1909-1975)<br />
The New York Times with Index (1851-2014)<br />
The Philadelphia Inquirer (1860-2001)<br />
Pittsburgh Post-Gazette (1786-2003)<br />
The Wall Street Journal (1889-2000)<br />
The Washington Post (1877-2001)</div>
                    </div></li>
                        
                            </ul>

						<div id="s-lg-box-24643798" class="s-lib-box s-lib-box-std">
							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">What about secondary sources: journals and books that may contain, or reference, primary documents?
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-24643798" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									<div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-58975908" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-10"><li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-33949142" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.ebscohost.com/login.aspx?profile=ehost&defaultdb=31h&authtype=ip"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '33949142'});">America: History & Life with Full Text</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-33949142" class="s-lg-database-desc">The definitive database of literature covering the history and culture of the United States and Canada, from prehistory to the present. With selective indexing for 1,700 journals from 1964 to present, this database is without question the most important bibliographic reference tool for students and scholars of U.S. and Canadian history. Full text journals include American Historical Review, American 19th Century History, American Studies, Early American Studies, Journal of American Culture, Journal of American History, Journal of Early American History, and Journal of Military History.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-33949143" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.ebscohost.com/login.aspx?profile=ehost&defaultdb=a9h&authtype=ip"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '33949143'});">Academic Search Complete</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-33949143" class="s-lg-database-desc">A database of both scholarly and popular publications in a wide variety of disciplines and subject areas, including psychology.  Most full text is linked directly in the database; otherwise use the Check for Full Text buttons to check for full text online or in the library.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-58975910" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-2"><li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-56498984" class=""><span><a href="http://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/advancedsearch" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '56498984'});">Encompass Search</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-56498984" class="s-lg-link-desc">Encompass Search searches for books and other resources, first in our own library, and then beyond, in other research libraries.</div>
			</div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-56498985" class=""><span><a href="http://scholar.google.com/" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '56498985'});">Google Scholar</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-56498985" class="s-lg-link-desc">May be used in conjunction with interlibrary loan. Access to full text is maximized by searching from the Muhlenberg network.</div>
			</div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-56498986" class=""><span><a href="http://books.google.com" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '56498986'});">Google Books</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-56498986" class="s-lg-link-desc">Google Books is a rich source of full-text documents (books and other materials) published prior to 1923 that are now in the public domain.</div>
			</div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-58975913" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-10"><li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-33949147" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://www.jstor.org/"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '33949147'});">JSTOR</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-33949147" class="s-lg-database-desc">A substantive archive of about 3,000 full text scholarly journals plus scholarly books in the arts, humanities, social sciences and biological sciences. As an archive, there is typically a lag time of 3-5 years from publication until new issues appear in JSTOR. Journals include core titles from a variety of disciplines: history, economics, biology, and more.</div>
                    </div></li>
