			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2015/03/st-patricks-day/" rel="bookmark">St. Patricks Day!</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2015/03/st-patricks-day/" title="9:43 am" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">March 22, 2015</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/felisa/" title="View all posts by Felisa W">Felisa W</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>Since St. Paddy&#8217;s Day landed on a Tuesday this year, all the college festivities occurred the Saturday before and then the Saturday afterwards. On Saturday, everyone was up from 8am. Even I was up to join in on the fun. It&#8217;s funny to think how easily I got up at 8am to be with my friends, but getting me out of bed for my 9:30am class is still incredibly hard to do. I pre-planned the lunch for my friends since I knew no one would want to go to the dining hall, and by 1pm, we had chicken, scalloped potatoes, 2 different kinds of dips, a veggie platter, cupcakes and cookies waiting for everyone. I cooked for 5 hours the evening before to make sure everything would be prepared on time but it was worth it! This weekend there will be a parade in Allentown which I will be attending as well (pictures will come later).</p>
<p>I hope everyone had a safe and fun-filled St. Patricks Day weekend!</p>
<p>Until next time,</p>
<p>Felisa</p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2015/03/media-communications-alumni-week/" rel="bookmark">Media Communications Alumni Week</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2015/03/media-communications-alumni-week/" title="9:43 am" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">March 22, 2015</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/felisa/" title="View all posts by Felisa W">Felisa W</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>Every year the Media Communication department puts on an Alumni Week where they bring in recent and not so recent graduates to have hour-long presentations and talks with students. This week is a great opportunity to network and learn about the industry from those who took the same courses as us. I got to attend a few talks and learned about public policy, PR and marketing strategies, and even got the inside scoop about some political lingo. I don&#8217;t think I&#8217;ll actually ever use this lingo since I&#8217;m not as interested in politics as the speaker, but now I have it in the back of my mind, just in case!</p>
<p>I wish I got to attend more, but there will always be next year!</p>
<p>Until next time,</p>
<p>Felisa</p>

			<div id="post-4049" class="post-4049 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-admissions-blog">
			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2015/03/new-journey/" rel="bookmark">New journey.</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2015/03/new-journey/" title="9:42 am" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">March 22, 2015</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/aria/" title="View all posts by Aria">Aria</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>As you may assume I was very close to getting fired by Admissions because I haven’t written anything for awhile, just kidding. But I must confess it was because I was on spring break and my dad was visiting me from China, so I kind of went wild!</p>
<p>Anyway, now I&#8217;m suffering from the consequences of not studying early enough for my approaching midterm exams&#8230;I&#8217;ve been up until 4 o’clock in the morning for the last 3 days!  Honestly, I do not feel regretful. My life motto is play and study hard.</p>
<p>During my spring break, I took a road trip with my best friend, Andrea.  We drove together from LA to Tijuana, Mexico and then to San Francisco. The trip was so much fun.  We basically ate the entire time along our travels.  We found a magic/unforgettable pizza in Carmel!</p>
<p><img class=" size-medium wp-image-4056 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4431-225x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4431" width="225" height="300" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4431-225x300.jpg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4431-768x1024.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">This is the pizza, and yes, you will never know if you like it until you try it. It has lemon and cheese and the combination is absolutely amazing together!</p>
<p>I&#8217;d like to share more about my trip with photos.</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4222.jpg"><img class="  wp-image-4050 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4222-300x225.jpg" alt="IMG_4222" width="420" height="315" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4222-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4222-1024x768.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 420px) 100vw, 420px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The first two days in Tijuana it was raining cats and dogs but on the day we were leaving, Tijuana showed us this amazing rainbow and sunset.</p>
<p><img class=" wp-image-4051 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4247-300x225.jpg" alt="IMG_4247" width="416" height="312" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4247-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4247-1024x768.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 416px) 100vw, 416px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Our next stop was to Newport, a small town full of nice people and stunning views.</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4660.jpg"><img class="  wp-image-4058 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4660-300x177.jpg" alt="IMG_4660" width="490" height="289" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4660-300x177.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4660-1024x603.jpg 1024w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4660.jpg 1280w" sizes="(max-width: 490px) 100vw, 490px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">(Newport beach)</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">I&#8217;ve only seen this color in the sky in California!</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4449.jpg"><img class="  wp-image-4052 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4449-300x225.jpg" alt="IMG_4449" width="412" height="309" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4449-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4449-1024x768.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 412px) 100vw, 412px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">In Hollister, my friend and I took a glider ride.</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4421.jpg"><img class="  wp-image-4053 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4421-300x225.jpg" alt="IMG_4421" width="463" height="347" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4421-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4421-1024x768.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 463px) 100vw, 463px" /></a><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4424.jpg"><br />
<img class="  wp-image-4054 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4424-225x300.jpg" alt="IMG_4424" width="313" height="417" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4424-225x300.jpg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4424-768x1024.jpg 768w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4424.jpg 1224w" sizes="(max-width: 313px) 100vw, 313px" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Bob, our pilot, was such a good guy!  Before we left he even gave us two t-shirts as gifts.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">Thanks for making my day, Bob!</p>
<p><img class="  wp-image-4055 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4427-300x226.jpg" alt="IMG_4427" width="332" height="250" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4427-300x226.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4427-1024x772.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 332px) 100vw, 332px" /></p>
<p>After many days full of sunshine and warm weather, it was time to return to my home in Allentown.  Unfortunately there was heavy snow in New York which caused many flight delays.</p>
<p>Can you imagine leaving 78 degree weather in LA to experience winter again?  I&#8217;m looking forward to warmer weather on the East Coast soon!</p>
<p>Although my journey in California has ended for now, I am continuing my journey in Allentown.</p>
<p>Isn&#8217;t that what our lives are made of? A different journey &#8211; one after the other, growing along the way. So let&#8217;s hit the road and be adventurous.</p>
<p>See you in the summer!  Kidding! I will post more regularly from now on.</p>
<p>Hope everyone does well on their midterms!</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

			<div id="post-4069" class="post-4069 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-admissions-blog">
			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2015/03/spring-break-chronicles-3/" rel="bookmark">Spring Break Chronicles #3</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2015/03/spring-break-chronicles-3/" title="1:40 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">March 20, 2015</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/felisa/" title="View all posts by Felisa W">Felisa W</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>I know I haven&#8217;t posted in a while but things have been getting very hectic over. Over break I mainly hung out with the international students who stayed on campus. Luckily I had my work study to keep me busy, and then on top of that I also had work that I was able to get a head start on (guess I didn&#8217;t get that much of a head start since I&#8217;m still swamped).</p>
<p>I met up a few times with my friends to cook dinner together (yes, you heard correctly&#8230; I cooked. Surprisingly, The meals were actually kind of tasty and I did not burn down my suite. I also went go-karting in Allentown (S/O to Uber for coming to Lehigh Valley and giving students more reliable options than taxis to get around).</p>
<p>Here is the bagel pizza I made&#8230; super quick and yummy.</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4004.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-4070" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4004-768x1024.jpg" alt="IMG_4004" width="422" height="563" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4004-768x1024.jpg 768w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4004-225x300.jpg 225w" sizes="(max-width: 422px) 100vw, 422px" /></a> Look mom! I&#8217;m making pasta!</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4061.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-4071" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4061-768x1024.jpg" alt="IMG_4061" width="449" height="599" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4061-768x1024.jpg 768w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4061-225x300.jpg 225w" sizes="(max-width: 449px) 100vw, 449px" /></a> Go-Karting!</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4068.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-4072" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4068-929x1024.jpg" alt="IMG_4068" width="357" height="393" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4068-929x1024.jpg 929w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4068-272x300.jpg 272w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4068.jpg 1138w" sizes="(max-width: 357px) 100vw, 357px" /></a> I felt like I should&#8217;ve been in a Fast and Furious movie!</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4066.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-4073" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4066-577x1024.jpg" alt="IMG_4066" width="359" height="636" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4066-577x1024.jpg 577w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4066-169x300.jpg 169w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4066.jpg 640w" sizes="(max-width: 359px) 100vw, 359px" /></a></p>
<p>Dinner with my friends!<br />
<a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4074.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-4074" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4074-768x1024.jpg" alt="IMG_4074" width="308" height="411" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4074-768x1024.jpg 768w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_4074-225x300.jpg 225w" sizes="(max-width: 308px) 100vw, 308px" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Although many students were not on campus and it was incredibly quiet, I enjoyed my Spring Break. I was able to relax, watch Netflix all day and the GQ was even open so it provided me with more food options. I also had my job to keep me busy and work to do, so that helped as well. Next Spring Break I probably won&#8217;t stay on campus again, it&#8217;ll be my Senior year so maybe I&#8217;ll go to Cancun or hit Miami Beach, who knows!</p>
<p>Until next time,</p>
<p>Felisa</p>
