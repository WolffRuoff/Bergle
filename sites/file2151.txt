		<p style="font-weight: 400"><strong>Digital Media Assistant, Communications, Office of Advancement</strong></p>
<p style="font-weight: 400"><strong>Muhlenberg College, Summer and/or Fall 2019</strong></p>
<p style="font-weight: 400"><strong><u>On-site Internship Advisor:</u></strong> Heather Lavin ’08, Senior Associate Director of Advancement Communication</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400"><strong><u>Goals</u></strong></p>
<p style="font-weight: 400">The goals of Advancement’s Digital Media Assistant are to:</p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Gain an understanding of social media marketing and digital communication strategies.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Develop a comprehensive content calendar for Muhlenberg Alumni Channels on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Provide student perspective in the development of young alumni programs and social media marketing.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Develop comprehensive communication campaigns that engage our online audience.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Learn to evaluate digital communications through analysis including social media insights and Google Analytics.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Execute social media marketing plan for events and mini-campaigns, including real-time photo sharing during the weekend.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Support the Sen. Associate Director of Communications in creating website content, news articles and/or emails. Training provided.</li>
</ul>
<p style="font-weight: 400"><strong><u>Job Duties</u></strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Interviews in April-summer; internship begins the first week of classes in the fall.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Attend weekly meetings with the Sen. Associate Director of Alumni Relations to discuss strategy, ideas, and project feedback</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Create and implement social media marketing plans for regional alumni events, campus and alumni. This includes marketing design, social networking updates and scheduling.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Submit regular social media analytics and monthly overviews of online activity.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Archiving for our online media through collecting old and current campus photos, documents, Weekly articles and yearbook photos.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">Write short news stories and content for MuhlenbergConnect.com, our alumni website.</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400">End of semester presentation to the Office of Advancement staff on alumni social media activity, strategy, future goals and recommendations.</li>
</ul>
<p style="font-weight: 400">Work 10-12 hours per week to meet the Media &amp; Comm. required time.</p>

<p><strong>The Muhlenberg College Dining Services</strong> is offering a (PAID) internship in for a student seeing experience or a future career in marketing, public relations, event planning, food and beverage or hospitality. The Marketing Intern will report to the Marketing Manager and work closely with the dining team at Muhlenberg College<br />

<p>Position Responsibilities and Operation Procedures:</p>
<ul>
<li>Layout and design with print and digital media including, but not limited to; brochures, magazines, posters, newspaper ads, digital signage, web ads, etc.</li>

<li>Managing social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram)</li>
<li>Filming and editing videos</li>
<li>Photographing events</li>
<li>Assisting with facilitating surveys</li>
<li>Writing articles for bi-annual magazine</li>
<li>Acting as liaison to Muhlenberg students and prospective students</li>
<li>Assisting with organizing and marketing dining events</li>
<li>Other duties and responsibilities as needed</li>
</ul>

		<h3><a href="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/12/idreammachine.png"><img class="alignleft wp-image-1811" src="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/12/idreammachine-300x100.png" alt="" width="338" height="120" /></a>Film &amp; Entertainment Marketing &amp; PR Internship</h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>Start Date:</strong> January 28th, 2019 (or sooner)</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>End Date:</strong> May 10th, 2019</p>
<h4>About the Company:</h4>
<p dir="ltr"><a href="http://idreammachine.com/">iDreamMachine</a> is an award winning, cutting edge entertainment company with over 20 years of experience producing content across multiple mediums, based in the Lehigh Valley. iDreamMachine is finishing up post-production on their latest project, Billboard, a drama about a an heir to a struggling radio station (WTYT 960) who decides to host a billboard sitting contest to pull the station out of the ratings basement. The story is told from two perspectives, the movie focuses on the radio station owner and the web series focuses on the four billboard sitting contestants.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Our upcoming marketing campaign aims to engage independent artists in cities around the country. This includes, but is not limited to: hosting gigs for musicians, putting on live art competitions, promoting artists through short form digital content and hosting wacky contests.</p>
<h4>About the Position:</h4>
<p dir="ltr">iDreamMachine is seeking skilled and qualified entertainment marketing and public relations interns. The candidates must be passionate about film, music and the entertainment industry. Artistic candidates pursuing an education in marketing/PR/communications are preferred but not required. Candidates will work directly with a Senior Producer to curate, execute and market creative events/campaigns. Candidates will assist with day-to-day tasks in marketing, booking, digital-media design and media/sponsorship. This is a college accredited internship; if approved through the university.</p>
<h4>Expected Duties</h4>
<p dir="ltr">Day to day you will be responsible for many aspects of the creative process. This includes but is not limited to the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Marketing, event planning, and promoting with a heavy emphasis on marketing through online platforms, i.e. the creation of social media content, PR relationships with bloggers and digital news sources</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Participate in brainstorming for potential events and concerts with the team</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Coordinating with venues, artists, and other staff involved with campaign events</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Collaborate with iDreamMachine producers to offer ideas and provide feedback towards the execution of projects</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Requirements &amp; Qualifications</h4>
<ul>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Demonstrate strong communication and interpersonal skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Must have a strong interest in film, music, art and live entertainment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Extremely organized and takes accountability for assigned tasks</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Working knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite programs (Photoshop, InDesign, Premiere, etc.) is a major plus, but not required</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Ability to work independently and with a small team</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Creative and detail-oriented</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="ltr">Some late evenings may be required</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Apply</h4>
<p dir="ltr">Please email resume in PDF format to <a href="mailto:rwalsh@idreammachine.com">Ryan Walsh</a>.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>Job Type:</strong> Part-time Internship (unpaid)</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>Required Education:</strong> Enrollment at a 2 or 4 year college or university</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>Internship location:</strong> Easton, Pennsylvania</p>
<p><strong>Est. hrs/wk:</strong> 12 &#8211; 24</p>

		<p><a href="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/11/wmuh_logo.png"><img class="alignleft wp-image-1803" src="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/11/wmuh_logo-300x172.png" alt="" width="475" height="273" srcset="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/11/wmuh_logo-300x172.png 300w, http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/11/wmuh_logo-768x441.png 768w, http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/11/wmuh_logo-1024x588.png 1024w, http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/11/wmuh_logo-788x452.png 788w, http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/11/wmuh_logo.png 1051w" sizes="(max-width: 475px) 100vw, 475px" /></a></p>
<p><a href="https://www.muhlenberg.edu/wmuh/">WMUH-FM</a>, the radio station of Muhlenberg College, has two internship opportunities for the Spring 2019 semester.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Audio Production</h3>
<p>WMUH is seeking an audio production associate to produce, record and edit audio content for broadcast and online distribution. Current projects include working with Muhlenberg Theatre and Dance, Student Engagement and the Living Writers Series to create, engineer and air monthly programming. Knowledge of audio editing software and recoding techniques is preferred.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Working with the General Manager to establish and execute a calendar for interviews and programs</li>
<li>Recording and editing original programs for broadcast and distribution</li>
<li>Uploading content to Soundcloud</li>
<li>Preparing and editing syndicated content for broadcast</li>
<li>Creating legal station IDs, liners, and promotional spots</li>
</ul>
<h3>Web Designer/Developer</h3>
<p>WMUH is planning to revamp our web presence and is looking for a web designer/developer to help with wmuh.org. Ideally this site will be ready to go live to coincide with our annual membership drive which begins April 8, 2019. Working knowledge of WordPress preferred. Graphic design skills a plus.</p>
<p>Responsibilities include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Working with the General Manager and student executive board to establish site style guide and content requirements</li>
<li>Coordinating with Muhlenberg Instructional Designer to create site prototype</li>
<li>Creating and developing content for site</li>
<li>Updating site as needed</li>
</ul>
<p>For more information, or to apply, contact <a href="mailto:wmuh@muhlenberg.edu">Paul Krempasky</a>.</p>
