<p>Whether you realized it or not, if you study in the social sciences, chances are good that you have used something published by SAGE. Trexler Library provides access to all SAGE eJournals and now we are able to give you eBooks as well. Recently, the Pennsylvania Academic Library Consortium, Inc. (PALCI) and SAGE Publishing reached an agreement for a consortium-wide purchase which provides all PALCI libraries with access to approximately 5,500 SAGE Knowledge eBooks &amp; eReference titles. The SAGE Knowledge platform contains information suited to all levels of researchers, from undergraduate students starting their first research project, to experienced faculty members.  All eBooks can be accessed through the <a rel="noreferrer noopener" aria-label="library catalog (opens in a new tab)" href="https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/advancedsearch" target="_blank">library catalog</a>.  Click <a rel="noreferrer noopener" aria-label=" (opens in a new tab)" href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://sk.sagepub.com/books" target="_blank">here </a>to browse the new eBooks on the SAGE website.</p>



<p>The range of disciplines includes:<br></p>



<ul><li>Business and Management</li><li>Counseling and Psychotherapy</li><li>Criminology and Criminal Justice</li><li>Education</li><li>Geography, Earth, and Environmental Science</li><li>Health &amp; Social Care</li><li>Media, Communication, and Cultural Studies</li><li>Politics and International Relations</li><li>Psychology</li><li>Sociology</li></ul>



<p>The collection consists of the following content types:</p>



<ul><li>Scholarly monographs</li><li>Reference works including handbooks and encyclopedias</li><li>Professional development titles</li><li>Textbooks – core and supplementary</li><li>Major works series</li></ul>



<p>If you have questions or would like assistance using this or any library resource, please <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/research/ask-a-librarian.html">contact us</a> by email,  phone,  or in-person. Alternatively, use our <a href="http://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/c.php?g=698426">Subject Guides</a>.</p>
