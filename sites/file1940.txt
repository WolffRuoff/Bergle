				    <h2 class="post-title"><a href="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/?p=522" rel="bookmark" title="Alliance for Sustainable Communities: Editing, Publishing &amp; Media">Alliance for Sustainable Communities: Editing, Publishing &amp; Media</a></h2>
				    
				    <div class="post-meta">
					
						<span class="post-date"><a href="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/?p=522" title="Alliance for Sustainable Communities: Editing, Publishing &amp; Media">July 22, 2014</a></span>
						
						<span class="date-sep"> / </span>
							
						<span class="post-author"><a href="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/?author=615" title="Posts by acorbin" rel="author">acorbin</a></span>
						
						<span class="date-sep"> / </span>
						
						<span>Comments Off<span class="screen-reader-text"> on Alliance for Sustainable Communities: Editing, Publishing &amp; Media</span></span>						
																		
					</div> <!-- /post-meta -->
				    
				</div> <!-- /post-header -->
													                                    	    
				<div class="post-content">
					    		            			            	                                                                                            
					<p><a href="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2011/12/logo.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-529" src="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2011/12/logo.jpg" alt="" width="755" height="112" srcset="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2011/12/logo.jpg 755w, http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2011/12/logo-300x44.jpg 300w, http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2011/12/logo-150x22.jpg 150w" sizes="(max-width: 755px) 100vw, 755px" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>The Alliance for Sustainable Communities-Lehigh Valley </strong>has two internships that are especially appropriate for students with a strong interest in writing, editing or publishing.</p>
<div>The Alliance&#8217;s <strong>EDITING &amp; PUBLISHING</strong> internship focuses on publication of the Alliance&#8217;s annual directory <em>Sustainable Lehigh Valley</em>.</div>
<div>Every spring, the Alliance publishes the <b><i>Sustainable Lehigh Valley </i></b>directory of organizations that promote sustainable communities to make the public more aware about the critical issues of sustainability and ongoing efforts to be more sustainable. (You can get an understanding of the Directory’s content from our website, but the print edition also involves layout, graphics, typography, and printing considerations.)The editorial intern works as part of the directory team to:• Communicate with organizations and businesses to compile and update directory listings</p>
<p>• Help select essays and artwork; edit essays submitted for publication</p>
<p>• Layout and copyediting using InDesign professional publishing software</p>
<p>• Maintain schedules and other planning to ensure on-time completion</p>
<p>• Make sure the online and print editions are synchronized</p>
<p>• Assist with other detail tasks involved in publishing these resources</p>
<p>Interns will gain experience in creating and editing a publication, strengthen project management skills, and use of professional publishing software, and develop a strong understanding of the multiple elements of sustainability .</p>
<p>We are looking for a student who can take significant responsibility for this project, works independently, and who has a strong interest in sustainable communities. Your academic major is not as important as your interest and ability to undertake a variety of tasks and work effectively. Previous interns have come from a wide range of academic disciplines, including Media &amp; Communication, Professional Writing, Economics, Graphic Design, and Art.</p>
<p><b>Requirements: </b>A strong interest in promoting sustainability; attention to detail; good computer skills and strong writing and interpersonal skills. Previous experience with InDesign is not required, although good basic computer skills are important.</p>
</div>
<div></div>
<div>Another great opportunity is the <strong>COMMUNICATIONS &amp; MEDIA</strong> internship, which focuses on website &amp; print communications. It can include preparing informative leaflets &amp; outreach materials, articles for the website, and op-eds to submit to the newspapers.  While interns typically write on a variety of critical issues, they generally focus on one main issue so they can really get familiar with it.</div>
<div></div>
<div></div>
<div>For more information, click <a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/current-internship-opportunities/" target="_blank">here</a>.</div>
<div></div>
<div><b>To apply for these internships:</b>(1) Visit the Alliance website www.sustainlv.org to get a sense of our goals and current work; direct questions to peter@sustainlv.org;(2) Prepare a brief statement explaining why you are interested in this particular project and how you want to contribute and email your statement and a resume to peter@sustainlv.org.</p>
</div>
