<p>“The most important thing I did during my internship was to meet people and find out how many factors go into changing the way something is done.…  I learned how to find out about things that tend to be hidden, talk to people about things they would rather pretend do not exist, and solve problems that seem overwhelming at first glance.” (JT, Lafayette College)</p>

If you are interested in one of the possibilities listed below, contact Peter Crownfield as soon as possible. Some of this work can be done off-site, once the intern is familiar with the project and its goals. [see preceding internship for more information about the project] <a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/current-internship-opportunities/communication-%E2%80%93-climate-sustainability-for-teachers-project/">[More info]</a></p>
<p>▪ NEW Communication–Combined Heat &amp; Power [CHP] Project – This internship involves a blend of research, keeping up with developments in the project, and helping to prepare an effective proposal and presentation to management. <a href="http://www.sustainlv.org/act-locally/internships-with-the-alliance/current-internship-opportunities/combined-heat-power-chp/">[More info]</a></p>
<p>▪ NEW Sustainable Events! – Moving towards zero-waste, climate-neutral events. Will include working with specific events to make them more sustainable, probably including one on-campus event and one community-based event.</p>
<p>▪ Communication – Communicating sustainability concepts to the community at large is a challenge, given the flood of misinformation in the mainstream media. Special focus on school greenhouse gas emissions [GHG] — exploring the causes, meaning, and actions needed.</div>
<div class=""><br class="" /><br class="" /><b class="">Climate Action!</b> — Work with others to increase public awareness of possibilities for local action now to reduce greenhouse gas emissions [GHG].<br class="" /><br class="" /><b class="">Food, Health, &amp; Climate</b> — Food from the industrialized food system not only fails to support people&#8217;s health but actually undermines health and has a huge global-warming impact. This internship involves research, analysis, and activism to raise both public &amp; professional awareness, including developing presentations, fact sheets, and/or posters.<br class="" /><br class="" /><b class="">Economics &amp; Sustainability</b> — Many businesses, especially in the extractive &amp; energy industries, create huge economic and health burdens for society, but these costs are externalized; we will look at the problem and work to raise awareness of the destructive results.<br class="" /><br class="" /><b class="">Community Rights</b> –  We&#8217;ve seen that agencies established to ensure &#8216;environmental protection&#8217; have devolved into systems of regulations that actually permit ecosystem destruction and pollution levels that not only degrade the environment but create a direct threat to people&#8217;s health. We need to raise awareness of the public and elected officials about the importance of using a community rights approach — as the colonists did before the U.S Revolution! This internship is at the intersection of environmental policy, government, and public awareness and will focus on raising awareness and working with interested local officials.<br class="" /><br class="" /><b class="">Act for Change</b> – self-designed projects to protect or expand equality, freedoms, or human rights.</div>

			<span class="post-date"><a href="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/?p=1354" title="Center for Public Integrity paid summer internships">January 22, 2015</a></span>
			
			<span class="date-sep"> / </span>

	<div class="post-content">
		    		            			            	                                                                                            
		<p>The Center for Public Integrity is looking for ambitious undergraduate and graduate students or recent graduates for a 10-week, paid summer internship in Washington, D.C. The Center is a nonprofit, nonpartisan organization that produces original, responsible investigative journalism on issues of public concern. A motivating factor for our internship program is to help train and inspire the next generation of investigative reporters.</p>
<p>Interns will assist reporters in producing investigative stories and projects on a wide range of subjects that may include: money and politics, national security, government accountability, environment, financial reform, and health care. Interns with appropriate experience may be assigned to our digital reporting or data departments.</p>
<p>Qualified candidates should have some reporting experience, strong writing skills, and a desire to pursue the types of issues we cover. Demonstrated interest in investigative or entrepreneurial reporting a plus.</p>
<h4 class="psheading4">Experience and Skills:</h4>
<p><strong>Who should apply?</strong></p>
<p>We are looking for students (or recent graduates) with some reporting experience, strong writing skills, and a desire to pursue the types of issues we cover. Students possessing the following qualities are encouraged to apply:</p>
<ul>
<li>High quality analytical, communications, research, and writing skills</li>
<li>Excellent academic record, professional/extracurricular/volunteer activities, subject matter expertise</li>
<li>Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and efficiently in a fast-paced environment</li>
<li>Computer-assisted reporting, web development, multimedia, graphic design or other special programming experience should be noted in the application.</li>
</ul>
<p>Interns will receive payment of $4,500 for 10 weeks during the summer of 2015.</p>
<p>The Center for Public Integrity is committed to creating a pipeline of young journalists from diverse backgrounds with excellent investigative journalism skills. People of color, women, LGBTQ, and differently-abled people are strongly encouraged to apply.</p>
<p><strong>Deadline to apply: January 31, 2015 </strong>Click here to apply: <a href="http://publicintegrity.atsondemand.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=512612.viewjobdetail&amp;CID=512612&amp;JID=480243" target="_blank">http://publicintegrity.atsondemand.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=512612.viewjobdetail&amp;CID=512612&amp;JID=480243</a></p>
