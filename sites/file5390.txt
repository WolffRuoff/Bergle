								<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p>After two intense rounds of sifting through the 70 (that&#8217;s right!!! 70!!!!!!) transcriptions we have gathered for this year&#8217;s project, the sediment has finally fallen away to reveal specs of gold. Today the ensemble spent twelve hours, 1pm-1am, narrowing down the passages that struck us the most. We then brought the words into the space with us by printing them out and spreading them around the first floor of MultiCulti. After weeks of hearing from every possible perspective, tonight we began the next phase by introducing all the different voices into conversation with each other.</p>
