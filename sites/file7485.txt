My family and I went on vacation to Lake Placid, NY which is the site of two past Winter Olympics. Although it was freezing and the town was showered in snow, we had a lot of fun walking around the shops in town, taking the bobsled run, skiing/snowboarding and checking out the ice hockey rink where the U.S won the gold!!! Additionally, while I was home, I took a few trips to NYC and saw some home friends. Break was very restful, but also very busy!</p>
<div id="attachment_1796" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/64055_4298024964040_64031818_n.jpg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-1796" class="size-medium wp-image-1796" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/64055_4298024964040_64031818_n-300x225.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="225" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/64055_4298024964040_64031818_n-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/64055_4298024964040_64031818_n.jpg 960w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-1796" class="wp-caption-text">Mom and Dad before the bobsled run!</p></div>

This past week, many of my friends came to visit school before they leave for abroad in the Spring! I know a lot of students who are studying abroad in the Spring, and even though I am not going abroad, I plan on living vicariously through them by stalking all of their pictures! However, I am also going to have the opportunity to visit a couple friends in Dublin, Ireland in the Spring&#8230; so stay tuned for a blog post about my trip to Dublin!</p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2013/01/missing-muhlenberg/" rel="bookmark">Missing Muhlenberg</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2013/01/missing-muhlenberg/" title="4:34 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">January 15, 2013</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/ml24513/" title="View all posts by Megan">Megan</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>Hello! My name is Megan. I am currently a junior double majoring in Media Communications and Dance. This semester, instead of returning to Muhlenberg, I will be studying abroad in beautiful Dublin, Ireland. I am participating in Muhlenberg’s Media Communications Dublin program. During my 4-month stay at Dublin City University, I will be taking 3 communication courses (one taught by Muhlenberg’s own Dr. Sullivan). A unique aspect of this program is the opportunity to complete an internship for the last 2 months of our stay. I am very excited to say that I will be the Marketing and Programming Intern for Dublin Dance Festival. This internship is the perfect fusion of my interest in communications and dance. I could not be more excited and thankful for this opportunity!</p>
<p>As sad and jealous as I am to read the Facebook statuses of my friends as they excitedly return home to campus, I am thrilled by the chance to explore Europe this semester! I am even more delighted to take all those reading along for the journey! So stay tuned for next week as I tackle the challenge of attempting to pack 4 months of my life into ONE suitcase… should be no big deal, right?</p>

			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2013/01/home-for-the-holidays/" rel="bookmark">Home for the Holidays</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2013/01/home-for-the-holidays/" title="10:13 am" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">January 8, 2013</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/db240243/" title="View all posts by Dani">Dani</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>This holiday season has been a really fun one. It has been a time for family, friends and tradition. My sister and I put up the annual Christmas tree together while listening to our favorite holiday tunes.</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/xmas-tree.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-1673 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/xmas-tree-e1357626125147-225x300.jpg" alt="" width="225" height="300" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/xmas-tree-e1357626125147-225x300.jpg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/xmas-tree-e1357626125147.jpg 480w" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" /></a></p>
<p>This break I have also been able to break from tradition and start to explore new things. I live close enough to DC that I was able to go exploring some more in such a wonderful city. I was able to go with my sister and some friends down to the Hirshhorn to see an awesome exhibit and visit the Christmas tree in the Smithsonian Castle. I also attempted to bake this break. For those who know me, my baking skills are not so stellar, but since I have certain allergies, I have been learning to bake pastries that I can eat and have been having fun doing it.</p>
<div id="attachment_1671" style="width: 235px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/xmas-at-smithson-e1357626065489.jpg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-1671" class="size-medium wp-image-1671" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/xmas-at-smithson-e1357626065489-225x300.jpg" alt="" width="225" height="300" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/xmas-at-smithson-e1357626065489-225x300.jpg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/xmas-at-smithson-e1357626065489.jpg 480w" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-1671" class="wp-caption-text">My friends and I at the Smithsonian Christmas tree</p></div>
<div id="attachment_1672" style="width: 235px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/chairs-on-chairs.jpg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-1672" class="size-medium wp-image-1672" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/chairs-on-chairs-e1357626225304-225x300.jpg" alt="" width="225" height="300" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/chairs-on-chairs-e1357626225304-225x300.jpg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/chairs-on-chairs-e1357626225304.jpg 480w" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-1672" class="wp-caption-text">Ai Wei Wei&#8217;s Chairs piece at the Hirshhorn</p></div>
<div id="attachment_1674" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/choclate-baking-pic.jpg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-1674" class="size-medium wp-image-1674" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/choclate-baking-pic-e1357626287362-300x225.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="225" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/choclate-baking-pic-e1357626287362-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/01/choclate-baking-pic-e1357626287362.jpg 640w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-1674" class="wp-caption-text">Egg-free chocolate cupcakes with homemade chocolate frosting</p></div>
<p>It is also a new year: 2013. I am about to embark on my last semester of college, but hopefully I am already off to a good start before it has even begun. I am willing to try new things. By the end of this year, I will be in a new place with a new job meeting new people, so why shouldn’t I start the year doing some new things now. This is the year to embrace change. It could lead to amazing things.</p>
