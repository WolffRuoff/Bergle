				<p style="text-align: center;">​<a id="WhatIs" name="WhatIs"></a><span id="docs-internal-guid-4dad1540-7fff-6499-9d0e-ccfb28f0daf3"></span><span style="font-size:20px;"><span id="docs-internal-guid-4dad1540-7fff-6499-9d0e-ccfb28f0daf3"><span style="font-family: Arial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"><span id="docs-internal-guid-ad82f4ce-7fff-8afb-04d3-ff659dd16d4a"><span style="font-family: Arial; color: rgb(17, 17, 17); background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">What is</span><span style="font-family: Arial; color: rgb(17, 17, 17); background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: italic; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"> Encompass Search</span><span style="font-family: Arial; color: rgb(17, 17, 17); background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">?</span></span></span></span></span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height:1.2;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt;"><span id="docs-internal-guid-4dad1540-7fff-6499-9d0e-ccfb28f0daf3"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"><span id="docs-internal-guid-58d5ecfe-7fff-7b51-4d8a-98ad2b551fe1"></span></span></span></p>

<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;">&zwnj;<em>Encompass Search</em> is more than a typical &lsquo;Library Catalog&rsquo;-- it is a unified discovery tool powered by the OCLC WorldCat Database. Through one simple search box, it allows you to find information by searching through a massive collection of books, scholarly journals, newspapers and magazines, dissertations, conference proceedings, music, video, maps, and more. Rather than searching in multiple separate databases, <em>Encompass Search</em> helps you find reliable resources from many catalogs and databases quickly and easily, all from a single search box!</span></p>

<p dir="ltr"><strong><span style="font-size:14px;">A single <em>Encompass Search</em> includes:</span></strong></p>

<ul>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;">Over 400,000 physical items in Trexler Library&rsquo;s holdings;</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;">Over 600,000 electronic resources (eBooks, video, databases, etc.) subscribed to or held by Trexler Library;</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;">Millions of links to individual scholarly journal articles, dissertations, conference proceedings, database articles, book reviews, etc. accessible through Trexler Library subscriptions;</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;">Thousands of out of print books in the public domain through the HathiTrust Digital Library;</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;">Millions of additional books and articles outside of our library--but available by request through Interlibrary Loan (ILL).</span></p>
	</li>
</ul>

<p dir="ltr" style="line-height:1.2;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt;"><span><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: transparent; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"></span></span></p>

<p dir="ltr" style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; line-height: 1.2; padding: 0pt 0pt 8pt;"><span id="docs-internal-guid-ad82f4ce-7fff-8afb-04d3-ff659dd16d4a"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; color: rgb(17, 17, 17); background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"><a id="WhenUse" name="WhenUse"></a>When should I use </span><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; color: rgb(17, 17, 17); background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-style: italic; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">Encompass Search</span><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; color: rgb(17, 17, 17); background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;">?</span></span></p>

<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;"><em>Encompass Search</em> allows you to quickly search, find, and access reliable and credible library content. In addition to locating books and other materials in Trexler Library&rsquo;s local holdings, <em>Encompass Search</em> is ideal for:</span></p>

<ul>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;"><strong>Researching paper topics</strong> by revealing a broad overview of what&#39;s &quot;out there&quot; in the scholarly literature;</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;"><strong>Finding representative, high-quality&nbsp;sources</strong> in various formats-- by filtering using the <strong>&lsquo;peer-review limiter&rsquo;&nbsp;</strong></span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size:14px;"><strong>Locating interdisciplinary information</strong> by drawing on databases and indexes beyond resources you already know about-- even beyond Trexler Library&rsquo;s holdings!</span></p>
	</li>
</ul>
