				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2014/04/baseball/" title="4:56 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">April 17, 2014</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/mattlawsky12/" title="View all posts by Matt Lawsky">Matt Lawsky</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>It was another big weekend for our baseball team. We went to Franklin and Marshall on Saturday and left with a sweep. This puts us at 6-3, which is good for second place in the conference. Our goal is to be in the top 4 so that we make the playoffs, which is only a few weeks away.</p>
<p>This is the half way point for our season. We still have 9 games left to play and some very tough teams on our schedule. Hopefully we can continue to play well and win enough games to get us in. After two tough seasons the last two years it is really nice to be relevant and right in the middle of the conference. It is not fun when you have to play games at the end of the year that don&#8217;t mean anything. At least we know that we still have a lot of meaningful games left to play.</p>
