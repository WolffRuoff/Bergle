                <div id="primary">
        <p>Trexler Library's archive holds thousands of original photographs depicting Muhlenberg College and its students, faculty, and administration throughout the late-19th and 20th centuries. These images are part of the Muhlenberg College Collection. The majority of the photographs were taken by the Public Relations department and were used in the <em>Ciarla</em>, Muhlenberg's yearbook, as well as in the alumni magazine and other outlets. Over 2,500  photographs have been digitized and are being added to the digital archive weekly.</p>
