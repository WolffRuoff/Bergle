                            <h1>Career Plan</h1>
                                <div class="new-text"><p><span style="font-weight: 400"> I am a dancer trained in modern, ballet, jazz, jazztap, ariel, and improvisation skills pursuing interest in performance, choreography, and arts administration.  In the future, I wish to become a professional dancer in a modern company, present my own choreography, and eventually do work in administration within an arts setting in my older years. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">I will be receiving my BA in Dance and Business this May. I am currently in the process of auditioning for a variety of dance companies as well as applying to present my choreography at a number of festivals throughout the country. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Some things I see happening hopefully in my near future include, performing professionally, choreographing, becoming a teacher or instructor of dance, marketing for dancers, managing for dancers, and stage management for production.</span></p>
<p>I believe my short term planing steps and the actions I have taken in my undergraduate experience have well prepared my for my field of interest. In the past four years I have p<span style="font-weight: 400">articipated as an active member in the Muhlenberg Dance Association including volunteering for department events, t</span><span style="font-weight: 400">ake master classes with professional company members when they visit campus, performed in Moving Stories, Master Choreographers, and Dance Emerge which are three mainstage concerts that happen each year. I have attended American Dance Festival in Durham, North Carolina where I have worked with diverse groups of people. While at this festival I had the opportunity to both choreograph and perform work. I have travelled abroad to Italy to work with artists with different backgrounds than my own. I have choreographed five different works in the past four years; one of which has been selected to be presented at a American College Dance Association conference. </span></p>
<p>One of my long term goals is to co-found a dance company with my collaborator Heather Dutton. To do so, I feel that first I need experience performing within a company to understand the ways in which they operate.</p>
