								<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p>Welcome to my food blog! I have to tell you a secret.  Besides being a full time student, I am also a chef! Hahahaha, I&#8217;m not kidding&#8230;I just discovered my cooking talent last Sunday.</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5378.jpg"><img class=" size-medium wp-image-4159 aligncenter" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5378-300x225.jpg" alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" width="300" height="225" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5378-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5378.jpg 853w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></p>
<p>Last weekend I made hot pot with my friends. It was sooooo good!  What I love most about hot pot is that it always brings together a bunch of good friends to talk while the food is cooking.  It goes round and round.</p>
<p><img class="  wp-image-4163 alignleft" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5377-300x225.jpg" alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" width="243" height="182" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5377-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5377.jpg 853w" sizes="(max-width: 243px) 100vw, 243px" /><br />
Yummmm!</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5380.jpg"><img class=" size-medium wp-image-4161 alignright" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5380-300x225.jpg" alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" width="300" height="225" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5380-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5380-1024x768.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a></p>

<p>I hope after you see my photos, you will be hungry.  Because I can&#8217;t wait to share my recipe with you.</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>First, go to a Chinese food market and buy a hot pot soup base called &#8220;haidilao（海底捞).&#8221;  Or you can choose a different flavor, but I highly recommend a spicy one.</p>
<p><img class="  wp-image-4158 alignleft" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5382-225x300.jpg" alt="Processed with VSCOcam with f2 preset" width="219" height="292" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5382-225x300.jpg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2015/03/IMG_5382-768x1024.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 219px) 100vw, 219px" /><br />
Then just throw anything you like into the pot and let it cook! Believe me it is easy, simple, and super delicious!</p>
