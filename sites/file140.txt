                    <div id="s-lg-content-46329108" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.alexanderstreet.com/gltv"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '46329108'});">LGBT Studies in Video</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-46329108" class="s-lg-database-desc">LGBT Studies in Video is a cinematic survey of the lives of lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender people as well as the cultural and political evolution of the LGBT community.                     </div></li>

                    </div></li>

                    <div id="s-lg-content-46329664" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.alexanderstreet.com/feat"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '46329664'});">New World Cinema Independent Features & Shorts, 1990-present</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-46329664" class="s-lg-database-desc">includes over 200 full-length feature films from leading independent distributors such as Kino Lorber, First Run Features, Film Movement, and Global Lens.                     </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-46329767" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.alexanderstreet.com/silf"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '46329767'});">Silent Film Online</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-46329767" class="s-lg-database-desc">This database covers silent features, serials, and shorts from the 1890s to the 1930s.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-46329902" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.alexanderstreet.com/ativ"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '46329902'});">Theatre in Video</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-46329902" class="s-lg-database-desc">Theatre in Video contains hundreds of videos, including documentaries and definitive performances of the worldâ€™s most important plays. Interviews with directors, designers, writers, and actors, along with excerpts of live performances deliver an authentic, behind-the-scenes look at hundreds of productions.</div>
