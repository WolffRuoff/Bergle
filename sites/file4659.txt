								<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p>Dr. Jeff Pooley, Assistant Professor of Media and Communication, is among a team of seven other researchers awarded a 3-year, $390,000 grant from France&#8217;s <a href="http://www.agence-nationale-recherche.fr/">L&#8217;Agence nationale de la recherche (ANR)</a>&#8211;the French equivalent of the National Science Foundation.  The project is entitled: &#8220;Cross-disciplinary research ventures in postwar American social science: Five case studies.&#8221;  The case studies include Chicago, Columbia, Harvard, Michigan, and MIT.  Dr. Pooley will direct the Columbia-based project.  The grant includes funds for archival research, a series of workshops and symposia and publications.  In this work, Dr. Pooley joins a team of leading historians in the fields of economics, sociology, and psychology.  Congratulations on this prestigious award, Dr. Pooley!<a href="http://mediacom.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2010/09/logo.gif"><img class="alignright size-full wp-image-416" title="logo" src="http://mediacom.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2010/09/logo.gif" alt="" width="150" height="81" /></a></p>
