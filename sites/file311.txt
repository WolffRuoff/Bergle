Now the <a href=\"https:\/\/www.gpo.gov\/fdsys\/\">U.S. Government Publishing Office<\/a> (GPO) and NARA have digitized the print volumes of the <a rel=\"noreferrer noopener\" href=\"https:\/\/www.govinfo.gov\/app\/collection\/PPP\">Public Papers of the Presidents<\/a> and made them available on  <a rel=\"noreferrer noopener\" href=\"https:\/\/www.govinfo.gov\/\" target=\"_blank\">govinfo<\/a>.&nbsp; &nbsp;There is one exception to the list of presidents included: President Franklin Roosevelt&#8217;s papers were published  by a private printer before 1957 and are not available as part of the <em>Public Papers<\/em> series.&nbsp; <\/p>\n\n\n\n<p>Each <em>Public Papers<\/em> volume contains the papers and speeches of the President of the United States that were issued by the Office of the Press Secretary during the specified time period. The material is presented in chronological order, and the dates shown in the headings are the dates of the documents or events. In instances when the release date differs from the date of the document itself, that fact is shown in the text-note.&nbsp;&nbsp;<\/p>\n\n\n\n<p>NARA released a webinar with further explanation of the <em><a rel=\"noreferrer noopener\" aria-label=\" (opens in a new tab)\" href=\"https:\/\/www.govinfo.gov\/app\/collection\/PPP\" target=\"_blank\">Public Papers<\/a><\/em> and how to use the digitized papers on govinfo. To view the webinar, click <a rel=\"noreferrer noopener\" aria-label=\"here (opens in a new tab)\" href=\"https:\/\/www.fdlp.gov\/public-papers-of-the-president\" target=\"_blank\">here<\/a>.  Trexler Library also has a print copy of the <em>Public Papers<\/em>. It\u2019s much larger than the replacement of any one particular tool.
