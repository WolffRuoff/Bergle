<p>The U.S. Government edition of the Mueller Report has the official name <a href="https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/GPO-SCREPORT-MUELLER/pdf/GPO-SCREPORT-MUELLER.pdf"><em>Report on the Investigation into Russian Interference in the 2016 Presidential Election.</em></a>   You may already have come across a link to the online copy of the report or to various other editions being made available for free or at cost. The version linked here is the original and official report (redacted copy). It is permanently available through <a href="https://www.govinfo.gov/">govinfo</a>   and linked through the library catalog.  When  searching for the report in Encompass Search (the library catalog) type in either the long official name or the popular name, &#8220;Mueller Report&#8221;. Keep the quotations marks around “Mueller Report” and the report will be among the top few search results.  Look for the report to be listed under its official name (not Mueller Report).</p>

<p> The Library also has received a copy of the paper edition of the report. The paper report is on Permanent Reserve and may be requested at the Lending Services Desk, SuDocs number J 1.128:R 92/OFFICIAL. The paper report is available for in-library use only. You will also find this in Encompass using the same search as above. <br> <br>The Encompass records for both the online and print editions are available <a href="https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/search?queryString=kw%3A%28%22Mueller+Report%22%29+AND+%28pb%3Agovernment+publishing+office%29&amp;databaseList=1708%2C638&amp;origPageViewName=pages%2Fadvanced-search-page&amp;clusterResults=true&amp;scope=">here</a>. <br><br>If you have any questions or need assistance, please stop by the Information Desk or Lending Services Desk or email refdesk@muhlenberg.edu. </p>
