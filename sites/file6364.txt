	 * @since 4.9.0

	 * @private

	 * @param {number[]} set1 Set of Emoji character codes.

          <h1 class="entry-title ">RJ Designated Courses &#8211; Fall 2020</h1><div class="entry-meta"><a href="http://rjfellows.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/?m=20200410" title="2:54 pm" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date updated" datetime="10 Apr, 2020">April 10, 2020</time></a> &nbsp;in <a class="" href="http://rjfellows.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/?cat=183392" title="View all posts in RJ Fellows Featured Courses"> RJ Fellows Featured Courses </a> <span class="by-author"> by <span class="author vcard author_name"><a class="url fn n" href="http://rjfellows.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/?author=1845" title="View all posts by rileyminkoff" rel="author">rileyminkoff</a></span></span> </div><hr class="featurette-divider __before_content">        </header>
                  <section class="entry-content ">
              <p><a href="http://rjfellows.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2019/02/RJ-Logo.png" class="grouped_elements" rel="tc-fancybox-group1493"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-1189" src="http://rjfellows.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2019/02/RJ-Logo-300x134.png" alt="" width="300" height="134" srcset="http://rjfellows.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2019/02/RJ-Logo-300x134.png 300w, http://rjfellows.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2019/02/RJ-Logo-768x343.png 768w, http://rjfellows.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2019/02/RJ-Logo.png 894w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a>The RJ Fellows Program engages students in courses that challenge and expand their understandings of and perspectives on change and leadership. While the course offerings vary semester by semester, all RJ Fellows must complete between their FYS and Senior Capstone 4 courses with the RJ designation.  These are courses drawn from across the curriculum and many also fulfill general academic or major requirements. Ideally, the 4 courses represent each of the 4 academic divisions–Math and Sciences, Arts, Humanities, and Social Sciences.  In selecting RJ courses, <em>no fewer than 3 of the academic divisions must be represented,</em> to foster breadth and understanding of multiple perspectives.  <strong>For first year RJs there has been a slight change in this requirement.</strong>  <strong>Please see my email or contact me if you have any questions.</strong></p>
<p>The following are courses offered in Spring 2020 that RJ Fellows can take towards fulfilling the requirements of the RJ Fellows program.  If a course listed here has multiple sections being offered as listed on Capstone, all sections apply.  Many of these courses also fulfill general academics or program requirements as indicated below.  <strong>Courses listed in the <a href="http://rjfellows.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/?page_id=554">Course Archives</a> have been reviewed and those that remain also count as RJ Fellows courses as describe above.</strong></p>
