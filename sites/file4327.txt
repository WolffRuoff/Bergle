			<h2 class="entry-title"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2013/04/paris-berlin-amsterdam-oh-my/" rel="bookmark">Paris, Berlin, Amsterdam, Oh My!</a></h2>

			<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/2013/04/paris-berlin-amsterdam-oh-my/" title="2:51 pm" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">April 25, 2013</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/author/ml24513/" title="View all posts by Megan">Megan</a></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
				<p>As we are past our halfway mark of being abroad in Dublin, our Dublin seminar with Dr. Sullivan has ended. However, we couldn&#8217;t have him go back to the States without a little thank you and good-bye party. The whole group got together for pizza. It was so nice to be able to see everyone from Muhlenberg together again! Since our seminar has ended and we have begun our internships, we do not have the opportunity to see everyone together on a regular basis in class. It was great to just sit down, relax, and talk about the semester.</p>
<p>Over the past 3 weekends I have traveled to Paris, Berlin, and Amsterdam. I had the most incredible time at each of them!</p>
<p>First, I went to Paris, France with my friend Cat. While there we also took a trip to Versailles. The gardens and palace there were beyond gorgeous. Each room was decorated more elaborately than the next. In the city of Paris, we went to several museums. I think my favorite place was outside of the Louvre. The day we went there were blue skies and the sun was shining, which is not common weather for Dublin, so I&#8217;m always super appreciative when I can find it. Cat and I grabbed lunch outside and admired the Seine River. The whole trip was really amazing.</p>
<div id="attachment_2193" style="width: 235px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/IMG_5064.jpg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-2193" class="size-medium wp-image-2193" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/IMG_5064-225x300.jpg" alt="" width="225" height="300" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/IMG_5064-225x300.jpg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/IMG_5064-768x1024.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-2193" class="wp-caption-text">SPOTTED: Mr. Mule with the Eiffel Tower.</p></div>

<p>In Berlin, my friend Molly and I met up with 2 of my friends from high school, Emily and CiCi. Emily is spending the year studying in Berlin, while CiCi was visiting from studying in Copenhagen. It was nice to be able to visit a city and to have the prospective of someone who has been living there. Molly and I also went on a 3 and half hour walking tour. I&#8217;ve found that if you have the right tour guide, walking tours can be really great. This walking tour was the best I have ever gone on! The entire tour was incredibly fascinating with really interesting stories about almost every building we passed. The last day we were in Berlin we came across a huge outdoor market filled with food vendors and vintage clothing. It was the sunniest and warmest day Berlin has had in awhile, so it seemed like the whole city was there. It was the perfect way to end the trip.</p>
<div id="attachment_2194" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/Berlin.jpg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-2194" class="size-medium wp-image-2194" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/Berlin-300x225.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="225" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/Berlin-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/Berlin.jpg 960w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-2194" class="wp-caption-text">Molly and I at the East Side Gallery on a windy day in Berlin, Germany.</p></div>
<p>This past weekend, I went to Amsterdam with Hallie, Leah, and Jena. The coolest part of this trip was our day visit to Keukenhof in Holland. It is a flower park that is only open for 2 months of the year because the flowers there are only in bloom during those 2 months. While the tulip fields were not completely in bloom yet when we visited, the rest of the flowers in the park were gorgeous. I have never seen more vibrantly colorful flowers before! It was definitely the highlight of the trip.</p>
<div id="attachment_2200" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/M-is-for.jpg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-2200" class="size-medium wp-image-2200" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/M-is-for-300x225.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="225" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/M-is-for-300x225.jpg 300w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/M-is-for.jpg 960w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-2200" class="wp-caption-text">M is for Muhlenberg! Jena, Hallie, Leah, and I at the &#8220;I AMsterdam&#8221; sign.</p></div>
<div id="attachment_2195" style="width: 235px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/IMG_5375.jpg"><img aria-describedby="caption-attachment-2195" class="size-medium wp-image-2195" src="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/IMG_5375-225x300.jpg" alt="" width="225" height="300" srcset="http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/IMG_5375-225x300.jpg 225w, http://admissions.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2013/04/IMG_5375-768x1024.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 225px) 100vw, 225px" /></a><p id="caption-attachment-2195" class="wp-caption-text">Flowers in Holland!</p></div>
