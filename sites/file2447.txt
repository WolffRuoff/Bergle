<p>Halloween is supposed to be a fun time of year to spend with friends, dress up in costumes, and enjoy the silly, scary, and spooky events that accompany this holiday. However, there are some spooky facts about Halloween and alcohol consumption that may scare you.</p>
<p>-Studies indicate that alcohol consumption goes up 25% on Halloween weekend</p>
<p>-Halloween and the Super Bowl are two of the top time periods throughout the year where alcohol purchases dramatically increase</p>
<p>-Drunk driving has caused Halloween to become one of the deadliest nights of the year</p>
<p>If you choose to party this Halloween, make sure to stay safe by following these tips:</p>
<p>-Eat before and during alcohol consumption</p>
<p>-Travel in groups and do not let friends travel alone</p>
<p>-Schedule your drinking and do not let strangers pour your drinks</p>
<p>-Remember that one standard drink is 1.5 ounces of hard liquor, 5 ounces of wine, or 12 ounces of beer</p>
<p>-Have a designated driver</p>
<p>Keep these facts in mind so that you and your friends stay safe and are able to have a HAPPY HALLOWEEN!</p>
