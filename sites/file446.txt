										<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p>On Saturday, 3 January 2015, there is a vendor-scheduled technology upgrade to several of the key systems responsible for library searching and requests through Interlibrary Loan.</p>
<p>During this time, the library&#8217;s website will not be impacted.  Also, users may continue to search the online catalog:  <a title="library catalog" href="http://library.muhlenberg.edu/search/X" target="_blank">http://library.muhlenberg.edu/search/X</a></p>
<p>as well as electronic databases: <a title="Databases A-Z" href="//trexler.muhlenberg.edu/research/databases-az.html" target="_blank"> http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/research/databases-az.html</a></p>
<p>During this upgrade, <strong>Encompass Search and Interlibrary Loan services will be unavailable from </strong><strong>12:01 am to 3:00 pm, U.S. Eastern Standard Time (approximately 15 hours)</strong>.</p>
<p>Real-time <a href="http://www.oclc.org/support/systemalerts.en.html">System Alerts</a> will be posted to the <a href="http://www.oclc.org/support/home.en.html">OCLC Support website</a> throughout the day.</p>
<p>While OCLC scheduled this maintenance event to occur during a slower time for most, we understand that this event still impacts many. We apologize in advance for any disruption this causes.  Thank you in advance for your understanding and flexibility.</p>
<p>&#8211;Trexler Library</p>
