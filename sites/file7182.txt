      <h1>Teamwork Skills</h1>
    </header>
  <p>For my marketing class I had the chance to work with a group and analyze the different marketing procedures for a company of our choice. My team chose to spend the semester looking at Under Armour. I completed thorough research that looked at how Under Armour markets their different products in order to succeed as a company with high brand recognition. Throughout this semester long project, my group and I learned about the history of the company, past marketing campaigns, and how they segment their market. The end of the project required our team to come up with our own advertisement for the company and gave us the opportunity to experience how marketers come up with different strategies to target specific audiences.</p>
<p>My team communicated consistently throughout the semester and set weekly meeting times to make sure we would successfully meet our deadline. These weekly check ins ensured that we were all on the same page with the assignment and allowed us to make sure we all were working together to get the work done in the most efficient way possible. From completing this project I developed better collaborative skills and feel more confident in my ability to work with groups to complete any given task.</p>
