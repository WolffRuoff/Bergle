							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Understanding Sort Order
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-22471931" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-50822598" class="  clearfix">
				<p dir="ltr" style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; line-height: 1.2;"><span id="docs-internal-guid-f8741837-7fff-8d70-5b4d-07d147d5312b"><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); background-color: transparent; font-weight: 700; font-variant-numeric: normal; font-variant-east-asian: normal; vertical-align: baseline; white-space: pre-wrap;"><a id="SortOrder" name="SortOrder"></a>Sort order</span></span></p>

<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 14px;">By default, your search will be sorted with items held by&nbsp;Trexler&nbsp;Library first (i.e. the &ldquo;Library&rdquo; sort).&nbsp; You can check the sort order (or change it) by using the &ldquo;Sort&nbsp;by&rdquo; dropdown under &quot;Refine the Search&quot; on the left side of the results.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-weight: 700;">The &ldquo;Sort by&rdquo; options are:</span></span></p>

<ul>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-weight: 700;">Library [default]:</span>&nbsp;lists&nbsp;Trexler&nbsp;Library holdings first, then uses a combination of&nbsp;Title, Author, Date, and number of holdings by institutions worldwide.</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-weight: 700;">Best match:</span>&nbsp;Ranks by Title, Author, Date and number of holdings by institutions worldwide with an emphasis placed on Title.</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-weight: 700;">Recency:</span>&nbsp;Ranks by Date, Title, Author, and number of holdings by institutions worldwide with an emphasis placed on Date.</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-weight: 700;">Author (A to Z):</span>&nbsp;Sorts by special characters or numbers, then A to Z by last name.</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-weight: 700;">Date* (Newest):</span>&nbsp;Sorts chronologically by publication date with newest items listed first.</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-weight: 700;">Date* (Oldest):</span>&nbsp;Sorts chronologically by publication date with oldest items listed first.</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-weight: 700;">Most Widely Held:</span>&nbsp;Presents an &quot;un-clustered&quot; view of search results with an emphasis on number of holdings by institutions worldwide and a combination of Date, Title, and Author.</span></p>
	</li>
	<li dir="ltr">
	<p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: 14px;"><span style="font-weight: 700;">Title (A-Z):</span>&nbsp;Sorts by special characters, then alphabetically by Title. Only the article &quot;the&quot; is ignored.</span></p>
	</li>
</ul>

<p><span style="font-size: 14px;">*</span>NOTE: Dates in the case of the&nbsp;OCLC&nbsp;database may not be original publication date, but rather the date of publication of the edition in hand.<span style="font-size: 14px;"></span></p>
