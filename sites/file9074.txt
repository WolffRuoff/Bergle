Good starting place for journals, and includes the full text of about 3000 journals. Limit to "peer reviewed" to find only scholarly articles. Back issues of journals begin as early as 1990. Recent issues may not be included yet, due to publisher agreement. Journals held in Trexler Library are marked accordingly.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-33949147" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://www.jstor.org/"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '33949147'});">JSTOR</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-33949147" class="s-lg-database-desc">A subscription database. Includes extensive full text, scholarly. Intended as a deep archive. Excludes current five years.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-36762411" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-2"><li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-33976205" class=""><span><a href="https://scholar.google.com/" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '33976205'});">Google Scholar</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-33976205" class="s-lg-link-desc">An open-access search engine that looks for content it identifies as "scholarly" on the free web. Emphasis is on books and journal articles. Intended as a "federated search" to search across disciplines (weighted toward sciences and social sciences). Not always full text. Be sure to check library catalog to see if Muhlenberg has access to the full text.</div>
			</div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-36762412" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-10"><li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-33949141" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.ebscohost.com/login.aspx?profile=ehost&defaultdb=30h&authtype=ip"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '33949141'});">Historical Abstracts with Full Text</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-33949141" class="s-lg-database-desc">***RECOMMENDED***<br/>A subscription database. Standard index for art history, with indexing and abstracts from early 1900s-present. Some full text.</div>
                    </div></li>
