<p><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;"><strong>Victory Flashes at Muhlenberg&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a target="_blank" href="http://library.muhlenberg.edu/search/?searchtype=X&amp;SORT=D&amp;searcharg=victory+flashes">T M952v</a></strong><br /></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;">Biweekly newsletter sent to students and alumni of the College<br /> serving in the war effort. Written by either Gordon Fister, <br />Director of Public Relations or John Wagner, Alumni Secretary.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; color: #000000;"><strong>[Log books of the Navy V-5 Unit] [manuscript]&nbsp;&nbsp; 13 vols.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <a target="_blank" href="http://library.muhlenberg.edu/search~S0?/X%28log+books%29&amp;SORT=D/X%28log+books%29&amp;SORT=D&amp;SUBKEY=%28log+books%29/1%2C48%2C48%2CB/frameset&amp;FF=X%28log+books%29&amp;SORT=D&amp;1%2C1%2C">E N327aL</a></strong></span><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino; color: #000000;"><strong></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;">v.1-6. Log books, Aug. 22, 1943-July 27, 1944.--v.7. Parcel post,<br /> telegrams, special delivery. v.8. Arrival and departure of civilians.<br />v.9 Airport log.--v.10. Airport extra duty.--v.11. Extra duty log book.<br />v.12. Extra duty (arranged by cadets)--v.13. Liberty book.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;"><strong>Muhlenberg College Alumni Quarterly</strong></span>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<strong>&nbsp; <a target="_blank" href="http://library.muhlenberg.edu/search~S0?/Xa%3Blumni+magazine&amp;SORT=DZ/Xa%3Blumni+magazine&amp;SORT=DZ&amp;extended=0&amp;SUBKEY=a%3Blumni+magazine/1%2C1391%2C1391%2CB/frameset&amp;FF=Xa%3Blumni+magazine&amp;SORT=DZ&amp;3%2C3%2C"><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;">T M952A</span></a></strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;">&nbsp;</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;"><a target="_blank" title="Muhlenberg Weekly" href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/collections/special-collections/muhlenberg-weekly.html">The Muhlenberg Weekly Archive Online</a><br /></span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;">&nbsp;</span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;"><a target="_blank" title="Letters Finding Aid" href="http://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/letters">Muhlenberg College World War II Correspondence Collection Finding Aid</a><a href="http://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/wwii"><br /><br /></a></span></strong><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;"><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;">The collection comprises correspondence to and from <span style="color: #000000;">262</span></span></span><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;"><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;"> Muhlenberg students and alumni. Carbon copies of Gordon Fister&rsquo;s and John Wagner&rsquo;s replies are included in the folders along with the original letters from the servicemen. Correspondence takes the form of letters, postcards, and V-mail. Folder contents vary from a few change-of-address postcards to detailed letters describing battles and experiences in the European and Pacific theatres, as well as many from men in U.S training camps and medical schools.</span></span><strong><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;"><span style="font-family: georgia,palatino;"> </span></span></strong></p>
