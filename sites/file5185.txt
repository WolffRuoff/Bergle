        <h1 class="entry-title">Quantitative &amp; Research Skills</h1>
      </div>
</header>  <div class="post-entry tc-content-inner">
    <section class="post-content entry-content " >
      <div class="czr-wp-the-content">
        <p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline">Local Arts Analysis</span></strong></p>
<p>As part of the Arts Administration course I have taken at Muhlenberg, we were assigned to research our hometowns and analyze whether or not it would be worthwhile to open an arts organization there. Our analysis was based off of four criteria: location, demographics, arts and culture resources, and surrounding communities. The full assignment can be found <a href="http://lioralgrably.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/04/Arts-Company.pdf">here</a>.</p>
<p>This assignment allowed me to understand the intricacies in choosing a location for an arts organization. As I am interested in potentially starting my own theatre company, this assignment helped to put that venture into perspective.I was able to think critically about the external environment of an arts organization. Since the external environment has such a big impact on an organization, it would be imprudent to only consider the internal environment (organizational structure, vision, etc.). Under the criteria specified, I was able to perform a <strong>SWOT</strong> (<strong>S</strong>trengths, <strong>W</strong>eaknesses, <strong>O</strong>pportunities, and <strong>T</strong>hreats) Analysis for my hometown and give a full recommendation on why I did not believe it was a worthwhile business venture. The experience and skills gained from completing this analysis will apply to my career post graduation.</p>
