										<content:encoded><![CDATA[<p>A library system upgrade is scheduled for July 8 &#8211; July 13.  During this time period, you might not be able to access full-text articles using the &#8216;Get It&#8217; button within research databases like PsycINFO and MLA International Bibliography or using Article Locator (located under the Journals tab on the library home page). You may also have difficulty accessing library-subscription full text through Google Scholar.</p>
<p>Fortunately, access to full-text articles within Encompass Search is less likely to be affected by the system upgrade. Please use Encompass Search as a work around to find full-text articles. Tip: When searching for an article, enclose your article title in quotation marks.</p>
<p>Contact Lending Services at <a href="tel:4846643500">484-664-3500</a> if you need assistance.</p>
<p>Also, please check this library news blog for updated information. As always, we appreciate your flexibility and support.</p>
<p>&#8211;Trexler Library Staff</p>
