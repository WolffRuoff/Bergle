			<h1 class="entry-title">Library Website Gets an Update</h1>

			<p class="entry-meta"><span class="posted-on"><span class="screen-reader-text">Posted on</span><a href="http://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/trexler_blog/news/library-website-gets-an-update/" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date published" datetime="2019-06-21T16:37:25-04:00">June 21, 2019</time><time class="updated" datetime="2019-06-21T16:37:31-04:00">June 21, 2019</time></a></span><span class="byline"><span class="author vcard"><span class="screen-reader-text">Author</span><a class="url fn n" href="http://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/trexler_blog/author/editor/">Library Resources</a></span></span><span class="comments-link"><a href="http://trexlerworks.muhlenberg.edu/trexler_blog/news/library-website-gets-an-update/#respond">Leave a comment</a></span></p><!-- .entry-meta -->		</header><!-- .entry-header -->

		<div class="entry-content">
			
<p>The library’s website has a new look! After receiving feedback from library users on campus and conducting a survey in April 2018 during our strategic planning process, we determined the site’s navigation and organization should be improved. We began planning for the update last June and completed user experience testing this past April. We continue to seek feedback about the site and are planning additional user experience testing throughout the year so that we can make further improvements.</p>



<p>While the site should still feel familiar to those who have been using it, we have updated site sections to include more information about all the resources and services we provide. Take a look at the new Spaces &amp; Technology menu which includes technology we have available for check out and in-library use and spaces that can be reserved for class projects or meetings. We have also added a Faculty Resources menu where we&#8217;ve grouped <a rel="noreferrer noopener" href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/facultyresources/" target="_blank">resources we think will be useful to faculty</a> together in one section and reorganized information to make it easier to find. </p>



<p>We welcome your feedback! Please submit comments or questions using the Comment form available <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/about/forms/comments/">here </a>and on the About Us menu. </p>
