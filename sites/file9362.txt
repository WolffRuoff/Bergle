			<div id="s-lg-content-50758571" class="  clearfix">
				<p><strong><span style="font-size:14px;">Don&#39;t forget --</span></strong></p>

<p><em><strong>Encompass Search</strong></em>&nbsp;uses <strong><span style="font-size:14px;">KEYWORD</span></strong> searching, so your search will locate records with <u>all of your search terms anywhere in the record</u>, and not necessarily in the order you want.</p>

<ul>
	<li>To search for a <strong><span style="font-size:14px;">KNOWN TITLE, enter your search in quotes</span></strong>.&nbsp; This will force Encompass Search to locate the terms together in the order you specify.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>You can <span style="font-size:14px;"><strong>combine terms</strong></span> using boolean operators like <strong>AND, OR, and NOT</strong>&nbsp;(be sure to capitalize them!)</li>
	<li>Once you have run your basic search, use the <strong><span style="font-size:14px;">FILTERS</span></strong> on the left-hand side of the results page to narrow your results by <strong>FORMAT, AUTHOR, LANGUAGE, or TOPIC</strong>.</li>
	<li>Use the <strong><span style="font-size:14px;">SUBJECTS</span></strong> hot links found within the item record under the &quot;View Description&quot; drop down. These are <strong>Library of Congress SUBJECT headings</strong>, and can be used&nbsp;to find additional resources on the same or similar topics, or of a similar <strong>GENRE</strong>.</li>
	<li>Use <strong><span style="font-size:14px;">TRUNCATION</span></strong> (i.e., shortening a word to its root or base form using a designated symbol) for words that might have more than one significant form, OR for terms that might have alternative spellings.​​</li>
</ul>

<p>For more information on Truncation or other Advanced Searching Techniques in Encompass Search see our &quot;<a href="https://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/usingencompass">Guide to Encompass Search</a>&quot;</p>

								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-50760055" class="  clearfix">
				<h4>To find a score of a particular musical work in Trexler&nbsp;Library&#39;s holdings,&nbsp;start with the main <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/" onclick="window.open(this.href, '', 'resizable=no,status=no,location=no,toolbar=no,menubar=no,fullscreen=no,scrollbars=no,dependent=no'); return false;"><em>Search&nbsp;box</em></a> on the library&nbsp;homepage.</h4>

<p><span style="font-size:14px;"><em>TIP: </em>It is usually best to combine the <u>composer&#39;s name</u>&nbsp;AND&nbsp;the <u>title of the work</u> as an initial search.</span></p>

<ul>
	<li><span style="font-size:14px;">Join these two pieces of information with AND (in capital letters)</span></li>
</ul>

<p style="margin-left: 80px;"><span style="font-size:14px;">Example: <a href="https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/search?databaseList=1708%2C638&amp;queryString=Beethoven+AND+symphony+no.+9&amp;clusterResults=true" onclick="window.open(this.href, 'Search', 'resizable=no,status=no,location=no,toolbar=no,menubar=no,fullscreen=no,scrollbars=no,dependent=no'); return false;">Beethoven <u>AND</u> symphony no. 9</a></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:14px;">Use TRUNCATION and WILDCARDS&nbsp;to search for:</span></p>

<ul>
	<li><span style="font-size:14px;">words or names that have more than one form, f</span><span style="font-size:14px;">or example: </span></li>
</ul>

<p style="margin-left: 80px;"><span style="font-size:14px;">enter&nbsp;<em>symphon<strong>*</strong></em>&nbsp;&nbsp;to find&nbsp;symphon+<strong><em>y</em></strong> or symphon+<em><strong>ies</strong></em></span></p>

<ul>
	<li><span style="font-size:14px;">words that have alternate spellings </span></li>
</ul>

<p style="margin-left: 80px;"><span style="font-size:14px;">especially true for Russian composers&#39; names like&nbsp;Stravinsk<strong>y</strong>&nbsp;or Stravinsk<strong>i</strong></span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:14px;">See the LibGuide on <a href="https://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/usingencompass/truncation" onclick="window.open(this.href, '', 'resizable=no,status=no,location=no,toolbar=no,menubar=no,fullscreen=no,scrollbars=no,dependent=no'); return false;">Truncation</a> for more information.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:14px;">See the &quot;<a href="https://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/musicscores/searchTerms">Musical search terms</a>&quot; tab in this guide for more information and strategies on search terminology.</span></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>
