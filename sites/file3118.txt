												<h1>Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
<p><a href="#faq1">Why do we need to switch from Groupwise?</a></p>
<p><a href="#faq2">Why are only Google Apps for Education and Microsoft 365 being considered?</a></p>
<p><a href="#faq3">How are privacy, security, and data ownership impacted by moving to a cloud-based service?</a></p>
<h2><a name="faq1"></a>Why do we need to switch from Groupwise?</h2>
<p>While Groupwise does offer many features, it is limited in its capabilities to just email and calendar. Many would like a more integrated experience to more effectively communicate, as well as collaborate with others, both within and external to the Muhlenberg community. Cloud-based, Software-as-a-Services (SaaS) options now offer much more functionality than is currently available through Groupwise.</p>
<p>Additionally, many on campus have already found alternative solutions because of their experiences with Groupwise or because Groupwise lacks a functionality that is necessary for their work. This includes: forwarding email to a different service, using a different calendar system, and using other applications to fill in gaps (such as chat and file-sharing).</p>
<h2><a name="faq2"></a>Why are only Google Apps for Education and Microsoft 365 being considered?</h2>
<p>The Project Team did review several different cloud-based, SaaS suites, but these two emerged as the front-runners. Services that were considered include:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://ccec.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/google-apps-for-education/" target="_blank">Google Apps for Education</a></li>
<li><a href="http://ccec.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/microsoft-365/" target="_blank">Microsoft 365</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.open-xchange.com/en/home" target="_blank">OX</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.zimbra.com/" target="_blank">Zimbra</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.zoho.com/" target="_blank">Zoho</a></li>
</ul>
<p>During the analysis of these particular services, Google and Microsoft offered the most by means of features, space, access, usability, and satisfaction. Several other institutions who were using one of the other options have also recently switched to one of these.</p>
<h2><a name="faq3"></a>How are privacy, security, and data ownership impacted by moving to a cloud-based service?</h2>
<p>Part of any contract or agreement that Muhlenberg would enter into would include terms of agreement that would address these concerns. Both Google and Microsoft also provide trust centers that provide detailed information:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="https://www.google.com/edu/trust/" target="_blank">Google Trust Center</a></li>
<li><a href="https://products.office.com/en/business/office-365-trust-center-cloud-computing-security" target="_blank">Microsoft Trust Center</a></li>
</ul>
