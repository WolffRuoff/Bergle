							<h2 class="s-lib-box-title">Getting Full Text Articles
                                </h2>
							<div id="s-lg-box-collapse-17250146" >
								<div class="s-lib-box-content">
									
			<div id="s-lg-content-38008852" class="  clearfix">
				<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, Verdana; font-size: 12px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 700;">GETTING THE ARTICLES IN LIBRARY DATABASES</span></span></span></p>

<ul style="box-sizing: border-box; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, Verdana; font-size: 12px;">
	<li style="box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">If you are in a library database with&nbsp;<span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 700;">Check for Full Text</span>&nbsp;buttons, click on &quot;<span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 700;">Check for Full Text</span>.&quot; This button will take you the full text of the article or tell you that the full text cannot be found. If the full text is not available from Trexler Library, request it via&nbsp;<a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/services/interlibrary-loan.html" style="box-sizing: border-box; background-color: transparent; color: rgb(51, 122, 183); text-decoration-line: none;" title="Interlibrary Loan Home">interlibrary loan</a>.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul style="box-sizing: border-box; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, Verdana; font-size: 12px;">
	<li style="box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">If you have citation information for the article (i.e., journal title, article title, publication year, etc.), type the information about your article in the&nbsp;<a href="https://muhlenberg.on.worldcat.org/atoztitles/search?#article" style="box-sizing: border-box; background-color: transparent; color: rgb(51, 122, 183); text-decoration-line: none;" title="Citation Help Page">article finder</a>&nbsp;on the library website. If the library subscribes to the full text, you will be directed to the article. If not, you can request a copy of the article via&nbsp;<a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/services/interlibrary-loan.html" style="box-sizing: border-box; background-color: transparent; color: rgb(51, 122, 183); text-decoration-line: none;" title="Interlibrary Loan Home">interlibrary loan</a>.</span></li>
</ul>

<ul style="box-sizing: border-box; margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 10px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, Verdana; font-size: 12px;">
	<li style="box-sizing: border-box;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">If Trexler Library does not have access to the full text of the article, search&nbsp;<a href="http://www.google.com/scholar" style="box-sizing: border-box; background-color: transparent; color: rgb(51, 122, 183); text-decoration-line: none;">Google Scholar</a>&nbsp;by article title to see if the author has made a copy freely available online. Look to the far right of the article&#39;s title to see if there is a link to a freely available copy.&nbsp;</span></li>
</ul>
