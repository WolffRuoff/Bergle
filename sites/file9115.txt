                    <div id="s-lg-content-33949143" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.ebscohost.com/login.aspx?profile=ehost&defaultdb=a9h&authtype=ip"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '33949143'});">Academic Search Complete</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-33949143" class="s-lg-database-desc">A database of both scholarly and popular publications in a wide variety of disciplines and subject areas, including psychology.  Most full text is linked directly in the database; otherwise use the Check for Full Text buttons to check for full text online or in the library.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div>
			<div id="s-lg-content-36169748" class="  clearfix">
				<p><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, Verdana; font-size: 12px;">Note:&nbsp; <a href="http://libraryguides.muhlenberg.edu/" target="_blank">Subject Guides</a> point you to recommended resources in each subject area. Look for the subject area related to your research interest and then for databases recommended for that subject.</span></p>
		   </div>
			<div id="s-lg-content-36169776" class="  clearfix">
				<p><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 700; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, Verdana; font-size: 12px;">POPULAR ARTICLES</span></p>
		   </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-59990762" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-10"><li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-51865981" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.proquest.com/internationalnews1?accountid=40980"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '51865981'});">International Newsstream</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-51865981" class="s-lg-database-desc">Provides full text news information from more than 660 of the world's top newspapers from all continents, including The Times (London), The Bangkok Post, Folha de Sao Paulo, El Norte, Financial Times, Jerusalem Post, South China Morning Post, Asian Wall Street Journal.  Does not include newspapers from within the United States.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-51865556" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.proquest.com/globalnews?accountid=40980"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '51865556'});">Global Newsstream</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-51865556" class="s-lg-database-desc">Provides full text access to current and recent archives of major daily news publications, regional, and local news resources in the US & Canada, plus over 800 more from around the globe. Publication types include newspapers, newswires, news journals, television and radio transcripts, blogs, podcasts, and digital-only websites.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
                            </div>
			<div id="s-lg-content-36169779" class="  clearfix">
				<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, Verdana; font-size: 12px;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: 700;">GOVERNMENT SOURCES</span><br style="box-sizing: border-box;" />
<br style="box-sizing: border-box;" />
NOTE: Take note of government agencies, government documents, and other government-related references in your scholarly and popular sources that may be helpful in directing your searching here.</p>

<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px 0px 10px; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, Verdana; font-size: 12px;">NOTE: Access to government documents from other countries via these resources depends largely on how available each country makes them.&nbsp; Language may prove to be another barrier.</p>
		   </div><div class="">
                        <ul id="s-lg-link-list-52185087" class="s-lg-link-list s-lg-link-list-2"><li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-49403625" class=""><span><a href="http://www.law.cornell.edu/world/" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '49403625'});">Legal Information Institute (LII), Law by Source: Global</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-49403625" class="s-lg-link-desc">The LII collection of world legal materials gathers, country by country, continent by continent, the Internet-accessible sources of the constitutions, statutes, judicial opinions, and related legal material from around the globe (excluding the U.S. material held in the LII's other collections). It also holds resources and document collections of International Law.</div>
			</div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-49403632" class=""><span><a href="http://www.washlaw.edu/forint/index.html" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '49403632'});">WashLaw Web: Foreign and International Law</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-49403632" class="s-lg-link-desc">Links to foreign and international legal resources and research tools. It's especially strong on finding legal materials from specific countries</div>
			</div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-49403643" class=""><span><a href="http://www.worldlii.org/catalog/215.html" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '49403643'});">World Legal Information Institute</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-49403643" class="s-lg-link-desc">Links to constitutions, laws, treaties, international agreements, and related resources by country.</div>
			</div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
			<div id="s-lg-content-57555591" class=""><span><a href="https://avalon.law.yale.edu/default.asp" target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '2',_st_content_id: '57555591'});">Avalon Project</a> 
				</span><div id="s-lg-link-desc-57555591" class="s-lg-link-desc">"The Avalon Project will mount digital documents relevant to the fields of Law, History, Economics, Politics, Diplomacy and Government. We do not intend to mount only static text but rather to add value to the text by linking to supporting documents expressly referred to in the body of the text."</div>
			</div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
