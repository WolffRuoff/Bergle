<p>The  LWV-LV seeks an editor for the newsletter. The Newsletter Editor will be responsible for the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee the production of the six monthly newsletters and three bi-monthly newsletters: February, March, April, May, October, November (Published by the first day of the month); the three two-month issues &#8211; June/July,</li>

<li>Working with website coordinator, produces printed version of Government Directory that the website coordinator edits and makes available online on an ongoing basis.</li>
<li>Works with PR Chair to send out press releases for the 8 Hot Topics Luncheons we put on every term.</li>

<p>The Website Coordinator Intern will assist in maintaining the content and format of the LWV-LC website. The Intern will also work with board members to maintain an up to date Government Directory online. This position requires that an individual be willing to learn use of the WordPress website-editing platform. Some specific duties include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bring the WordPress website up to standard.</li>
<li>Receive approved content from the President to be posted to the website. This content will include information for the public site (Hot Topics Luncheon, Government Directory, Voters Guide, LWV-LC events, articles, LWVPA events, membership and meeting registration, etc.).</li>
<li>Work with board members to update the online Government Directory after elected and appointed officials take office and whenever a change is made in the information contained in the Directory.</li>
<li>Work with board members when creating website version of the bi-annual Voters Guide.</li>
<li>Update board member contact information as new members are elected or appointed.</li>
</ul>
<p>For more information on this internship, please <a href="http://mediacom.internship.blogs.muhlenberg.edu/files/2018/09/PositionDescriptionWebsiteCoordinator.pdf">see the the attached information sheet</a>.</p>

<p>Do you want a summer job unlike any other? Is PR your passion? Are you ready to learn about the fast-paced world of agency life in a collaborative work environment?</p>
<p>Hornercom, a full-service public relations and marketing communications agency located in Harleysville, Pa., is seeking enthusiastic and hard-working applicants for a paid summer internship. If you go to college in the Greater Philadelphia area, or if you’re a student coming home to the area for the summer, come be a part of our award-winning team!</p>

Applicants must also be able to work a minimum of 25 hours per week for a minimum of eight consecutive weeks and must be eligible to receive college credit. Applicants should possess strong skills in writing and proofreading, as well as an understanding of time management and a commitment to meeting deadlines. Proficiency in Microsoft Office and social media platforms is required.</p>
<p><b>Pay:</b> Intern will be paid a stipend of $1,500.</p>
<p><b>To apply:</b> Please send resume and cover letter to <a href="mailto:info@hornercom.com">info@hornercom.com</a> with “Summer Intern” in the subject line. <i>Over-achievers, do apply.</i></p>
