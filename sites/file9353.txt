                    <div id="s-lg-content-46329276" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.alexanderstreet.com/shmu"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '46329276'});">Classical Scores Library</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-46329276" class="s-lg-database-desc">Classical Scores Library is a series of four volumes with a mission to provide a reliable and authoritative source for scores of the classical canon, as well as a resource for the discovery of lesser-known contemporary works. The collections encompass all major classical musical genres and time periods from the Middle Ages to the 21st century. With full, study, piano, and vocal scores, this comprehensive collection will enhance the study of music history, performance, composition and theory for a variety of scholars.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-53016340" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://metopera.org/season/on-demand/"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '53016340'});">Met Opera on Demand</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-53016340" class="s-lg-database-desc">Online streaming access to over 700 full-length Metropolitan Opera performances. Available to current students, faculty, and staff.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-46329410" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.alexanderstreet.com/opiv"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '46329410'});">Opera in Video</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-46329410" class="s-lg-database-desc">Opera in Video contains five hundred hours of the most important opera performances, captured on video through staged productions, interviews, and documentaries.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-58704845" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=http://www.rockandroll.amdigital.co.uk"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '58704845'});">Popular Culture in Britain and America, 1950-1975 (Adam Matthew)</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-58704845" class="s-lg-database-desc">Popular Culture explores the dynamic period of social, political and cultural change between 1950 and 1975. The resource offers thousands of colour images of manuscript and rare printed material as well as photographs, ephemera and memorabilia from this exciting period in our recent history. Explore our interactive chronology to learn a wealth of fascinating facts, take a tour of our visual resources, and watch video footage to experience the spectacular sights and sounds of the period for yourself.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        <li class="">
                        
                    <div id="s-lg-content-58706215" class=""><a href="https://muhlenberg.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://www.victorianpopularculture.amdigital.co.uk"  target="_blank"  onclick="return springSpace.springTrack.trackLink({link: this,_st_type_id: '16',_st_content_id: '58706215'});">Victorian Popular Culture (Adam Matthew)</a> <div id="s-lg-database-desc-58706215" class="s-lg-database-desc">Comprised of four modules, inviting users into the darkened halls, small backrooms, big tops and travelling venues that hosted everything from spectacular shows and bawdy burlesque, to the world of magic, spiritualist séances, optical entertainments and the first moving pictures.</div>
                    </div></li>
                        
                            </ul>
