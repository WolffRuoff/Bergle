<h2 class="boxtitle">Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<p class="q">What's my Login ID and Password?</p>
<p class="a">
<strong>Muhlenberg students, faculty, and staff:</strong> 
Use your Network Login ID and password.<br><br>  
For students, your Network Login ID is typically the part of your email address prior to @muhlenberg.edu<br>
<strong>(Ex., ab1234)</strong>;<br><br>
For faculty and staff, your Network Login ID is typically your first name and last name without spaces<br>
<strong>(Ex., henrymuhlenberg)</strong>.
</p>


<p class="q">I don't remember my Network Login ID and password, or my Network Login ID and password aren't working. What can I do?</p>
<!--<p class="a">Visit the <a href="#">Reset my password</a> page and reset your Network Login password.</p>-->

<p class="a">If you are unable to login, your OneLogin password may need to be updated. Please see our <a href="http://trexler.muhlenberg.edu/library/onlineresources/off-campusaccess/" target="_blank">instructions for updating your OneLogin password</a> and then try logging in again. If after updating your password, you are still unable to login, please use the folowing information to contact the help desk.</p>

<p class="a">
  <strong>Students</strong>:  Contact the Student Help Desk at (484)664-3566, OR<br>
                              Email the Student Help Desk at <a href="mailto:helpdesk@muhlenberg.edu">helpdesk@muhlenberg.edu</a>
</p>

<p class="a">                
  <strong>Faculty &amp; Staff</strong>:  Contact the Faculty/Staff Support Desk at (484)664-3566, OR<br>
                                         Email the Faculty/Staff Support Desk at <a href="mailto:support@muhlenberg.edu">support@muhlenberg.edu</a>
</p>

<p class="q">I'm not a student or employee of Muhlenberg College. Can I access electronic resources?</p>

<p class="a">

  some of our subscription database contracts require that off-campus access be limited to our 
  current students, staff and faculty.
</p>
